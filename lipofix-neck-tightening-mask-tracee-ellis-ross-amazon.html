Tracee Ellis Ross Uses Lipofix's Neck Tightening Mask

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Even if you are the type of skincare loyalist who remembers to wash their face after a long night out, the skin on the neck is incredibly easy to overlook. That being said, it's thinner and more sensitive, so it can show signs of aging faster than other areas of skin. It's a problem that can affect everyone — even celebrities who have entire glam squads. So when Tracee Ellis Ross shared a video of herself in a neck mask, the Instagram comments were ablaze with pleas for the product information.

In the Instagram video, makeup artist Molly Greenwald is seen applying the mask, to which Ross says, "Molly's really looking out for the gal… This is amazing… it feels so good." The actress and Pattern Beauty founder then asked Greenwald what's in the mask; the hero ingredients are familiar favorites — hyaluronic acid, niacinamide, and ceramides. Ross read the name of the mask, allowing an Instagram user to quickly identify it as Lipofix's Intense Lifting and Hydration Neck Treatment in the comments.
