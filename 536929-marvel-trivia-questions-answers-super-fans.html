110 Marvel trivia questions with answers for the super fans

Publish date: 2024-04-28

Think you're a die-hard Marvel fan? Think again. Marvel trivia questions and answers are tricky but will perfectly test your Marvel knowledge. From Avengers: Endgame to Iron Man, you will surely get tripped up on at least a few of these hard trivia questions.

Marvel Comics is a famous American comic book publisher owned by The Walt Disney Company since 2009. The Marvel era began in 1961 with the launch of Fantastic Four and other superhero titles. Today, Marvel’s well-known superheroes and supervillains include Spider-Man, Captain America, Hulk, Doctor Doom, Thanos and more.

Avengers trivia

The Avengers' original superhero team consisted of Hulk, Thor, Ant-Man, Iron Man and The Wasp. Captain America was found trapped in ice and later joined the Avengers team after they revived him. Here are some interesting Avengers trivia questions with answers to test your knowledge.

90s trivia questions and answers to bring back the memories

  • Who are the founding members and leaders of the Avengers? Answer: Iron Man, Thor, Ant-Man, The Wasp, The Hulk
  • What is the name of the powerful artefact that Loki uses to control minds in the first Avengers movie? Answer: The Tesseract or Space Stone
  • Who is the Norse god of thunder and a vital member of the Avengers? Answer: Thor
  • Which Avenger is known for his incredible archery skills and is named Hawkeye? Answer: Clint Barton
  • What is the name of the artificial intelligence created by Tony Stark to assist the Avengers? Answer: J.A.R.V.I.S. (Just A Rather Very Intelligent System)
  • Which Avenger is a highly skilled spy and master of disguise with a troubled past as a former Russian assassin? Answer: Natasha Romanoff, also known as Black Widow
  • What is the name of the android member of the Avengers who possesses superhuman strength, speed, and flying ability? Answer: Vision
  • Who is the Avenger with a scientific background who can shrink to the size of an ant or grow to gigantic proportions? Answer: Ant-Man (Dr. Hank Pym or Scott Lang)
  • What is the name of the highly advanced A.I. suit created by Tony Stark that allows him to become the armoured superhero, Iron Man? Answer: The Mark XLII suit
  • Which Avenger is a super-soldier enhanced by a serum and carries a shield made of vibranium? Answer: Captain America (Steve Rogers)
  • What is the name of the African nation ruled by T'Challa, also known as the Black Panther? Answer: Wakanda
  • Who is the Avenger with enhanced speed, often called the fastest man alive? Answer: Quicksilver (Pietro Maximoff)
  • Which Avenger is an expert in gamma radiation and transforms into a powerful green-skinned creature when angry? Answer: The Hulk (Bruce Banner)
  • What is the name of the Avengers' headquarters, a state-of-the-art facility in upstate New York? Answer: The Avengers Tower (also known as the Stark Tower in the Marvel Cinematic Universe)
  • Who is the Avenger with a genius-level intellect, a suit of high-tech armour, and a close friendship with Tony Stark? Answer: War Machine (James "Rhodey" Rhodes)
  • What is the name of the extra-terrestrial threat that the Avengers unite to battle in the first Avengers movie? Answer: The Chitauri, led by Loki
  • Who is the Avenger who can manipulate energy and create force fields, serving as the team's resident scientist? Answer: Scarlet Witch (Wanda Maximoff)
  • Which Avenger is a martial arts master and is also known as the "Master of Kung Fu"? Answer: Shang-Chi
  • Who is the Avenger who can control and communicate with insects, utilising them in combat and reconnaissance missions? Answer: Ant-Man (Scott Lang)

Marvel super hero trivia

The Marvel superheroes, including Hulk, Spider-Man, X-Men, etc., use their superhuman powers to fight crime and assorted villains, who often display superhuman abilities. Here are the easy Marvel trivia questions and answers about superheroes.

  • What radioactive arachnid bite gives Peter Parker his superpowers, allowing him to become the wall-crawling hero Spider-Man? Answer: Spider
  • What is the alias of Matt Murdock, a blind lawyer who fights crime in Hell's Kitchen using his enhanced senses and martial arts skills? Answer: Daredevil
  • Which superhero is known as the "First Avenger," having been enhanced to peak human condition during World War II? Answer: Captain America (Steve Rogers).
  • Which superhero possesses the ability to manipulate and control ice and snow, often working alongside his brother, the Scarlet Witch? Answer: Iceman (Bobby Drake).
  • Who is the mutant with retractable Adamantium claws and a healing factor? Answer: Logan or Wolverine
  • What is the name of Wakanda's African king and protector, who fights crime using advanced technology and enhanced abilities? Answer: T'Challa or Black Panther
  • Which superhero possesses superhuman speed and fights crime as the Scarlet Speedster? Answer: The Flash
  • What is the name of the brilliant scientist who can stretch his body into incredible shapes and sizes, leading the Fantastic Four? Answer: Mr. Fantastic
  • Who is the mutant telepath and founder of the X-Men, who fights for peaceful coexistence between mutants and humans? Professor X
  • Who is the blind superhero with heightened senses, trained in martial arts, and protects the streets of Hell's Kitchen? Answer: Daredevil (Matt Murdock)
  • What is the alias of the genius billionaire who fights crime using high-tech gadgets and an arsenal of weapons? Answer: Batman
  • What is the name of the superhero team consisting of Professor X's mutant students, dedicated to protecting a world that fears and hates them? Answer: The X-Men

Marvel cinematic universe trivia questions and answers

Are you looking for hard Marvel trivia questions to keep your friends engaged? Well, the Marvel cinematic universe trivia questions and answers are perfect. This is because the films are usually more detailed and complex than the comic books. Here are a few hard Marvel questions and answers.

  • What is the name of Tony Stark's assistant, who later becomes the superhero War Machine? Answer: James "Rhodey" Rhodes
  • Which Infinity Stone is housed within the Orb in Guardians of the Galaxy? Answer: The Power Stone
  • Who is the director of S.H.I.E.L.D., later revealed to be a Hydra operative in Captain America: The Winter Soldier? Answer: Alexander Pierce
  • Which character sacrifices himself by crashing a damaged aircraft into the Chitauri mothership in The Avengers? Answer: Iron Man
  • Who is the primary antagonist in Black Panther, seeking to usurp the throne of Wakanda? Answer: Erik Killmonger, also known as N'Jadaka
  • What is the organisation's name that experimented on Steve Rogers, transforming him into Captain America? Answer: Project Rebirth, later known as the Strategic Scientific Reserve (S.S.R.)
  • What is the name of Thor's home realm in the Marvel Cinematic Universe? Answer: Asgard
  • Which character is revealed to be the father of Peter Quill, also known as Star-Lord, in Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2? Answer: Ego the Living Planet
  • Who is the archer and master marksman who becomes an Avengers member under the codename Hawkeye? Answer: Clint Barton
  • What is the name of the organisation founded by Nick Fury to protect Earth from threats, as seen in various MCU films? Answer: S.H.I.E.L.D. (Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement, and Logistics Division)
  • What is the name of the technology company founded by Tony Stark, which became a significant player in the MCU? Answer: Stark Industries
  • Which villain becomes the Dark Elf leader seeking to unleash the Aether in Thor: The Dark World? Answer: Malekith
  • In Ant-Man, who is the original Ant-Man and mentor to Scott Lang? Answer: Hank Pym
  • What is the name of the advanced African nation ruled by T'Challa, also known as the Black Panther? Answer: Wakanda
  • Who is the actor who portrays the character Thanos, the primary antagonist in the Avengers films? Answer: Josh Brolin
  • What is the name of the A.I. system created by Tony Stark after J.A.R.V.I.S. is uploaded to Vision's synthetic body? Answer: F.R.I.D.A.Y.
  • What is the name of the extra-terrestrial species responsible for attacking New York City in The Avengers? Answer: The Chitauri

Marvel supervillain trivia

Marvel has many supervillains, including Thanos, Dormammu, Red Skull, Magneto, Doctor Doom and more. The fictional evildoers use their extraordinary powers towards nefarious ends. Here are some trivia questions and answers to test your knowledge and fulfil your marvel trivial pursuit.

  • What deadly metal is bonded to Wolverine's skeleton by the criminal organisation Weapon X? Answer: Adamantium
  • Who is the arch-nemesis of Spider-Man, often depicted as a criminal mastermind and founder of the Sinister Six? Answer: The Green Goblin, also known as Norman Osborn.
  • Which villain possesses the Cosmic Cube, granting them immense reality-warping powers? Answer: The Red Skull
  • What is the name of the alien symbiote that bonds with Eddie Brock? Answer: Venom
  • Which villain is a rogue artificial intelligence and enemy of the Avengers, often associated with the phrase "Ultron will return"? Answer: Ultron
  • Who is the ruler of the underwater kingdom of Atlantis and a frequent antagonist of the Fantastic Four? Answer: Namor the Sub-Mariner
  • What is the name of the villainous organisation led by the mutant Magneto, which seeks to promote the superiority of mutants over humans? Answer: The Brotherhood of Mutants
  • Which villain is the primary adversary of Daredevil, a blind lawyer who fights crime in Hell's Kitchen? Answer: Wilson Fisk, also known as the Kingpin
  • What is the name of the mad titan who seeks to impress the embodiment of Death by wiping out half of all life in the universe? Answer: Thanos
  • Which villainous entity serves as the living embodiment of the universal concept of entropy and opposes the cosmic entity Eternity? Answer: Entropy
  • Who is the technologically advanced villain who frequently battles Iron Man and desires world domination? Answer: The Mandarin
  • Which villain is the symbiotic offspring of Venom and possesses similar abilities, often serving as an enemy of Spider-Man? Answer: Carnage
  • What is the name of the villainous alien race that seeks to conquer and enslave other planets, led by the warlord Thanos? Answer: The Chitauri
  • Who is the immortal mutant manipulating historical events from the shadows and opposing the X-Men? Answer: Apocalypse
  • Which villain is the ruler of the Dark Dimension and often clashes with Doctor Strange over mystical matters? Answer: Dormammu
  • Who is the archenemy of the Black Panther, a supervillain with enhanced strength, agility, and a vibranium suit? Answer: Erik Killmonger
  • What is the name of the villainous alien race that seeks to harness the power of the Infinity Stones for their destructive purposes? Answer: The Skrulls
