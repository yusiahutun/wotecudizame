Tracee Ellis Ross Is Radiant in Carolina Herrera at the Oscars

Publish date: 2024-09-18

If anyone knows how to bring the glamour, it's Tracee Ellis Ross.

The Black-ish actress made a grand entrance on the red carpet for the 94th Academy Awards this evening. For Hollywood's biggest night, Ross wore a stunning scarlet gown by Carolina Herrera with a plunging sweetheart neckline, ruffled mermaid-style hem, and shimmery diamond choker necklace by NIWAKA.

The actress kept her glam for the evening fresh and classic, opting for her signature curls slicked back into a sophisticated low chignon, bold brows, and a matching matte red lip. Ross accessorized her awards show ensemble with simple pearl stud earrings and red pointed-toe pumps as well.

Jeff Kravitz//Getty Images

Ross shared a glimpse of her gown on Instagram before hitting the carpet, simply captioning the photo op, "Oscars, baby!"

The actress has never been shy about her love of fashion and has often expressed how embracing her personal style has meant more to her over the years.

"[Sharing my outfits] is a way to put joy in action for me. When I get dressed, I think to myself, 'Ah, I like her, I remember her!' She got lost a little bit," Ross previously told BAZAAR.com. "During the pandemic, I continued working and there was no question that I continued putting on my lipstick, putting on a beautiful top and earrings and necklaces and things like that, but to be going back out into the world and getting dressed for that—I missed that part of my life. … Style is one of my forms of creative expression, and I think beautiful timeless wearable pieces that hold special stories can act as an expression of who you are, but can also function in your everyday life."
