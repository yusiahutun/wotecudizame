Lil Romeo’s Early Life

Born as Percy Romeo Miller, Jr. on August 19, 1989, in New Orleans, Louisiana, Lil Romeo was destined to be a star. Growing up in a family with a rich musical legacy, both of his parents, Master P and Sonya C, were accomplished rappers. Moreover, he was the nephew of renowned rappers C-Murder and Silkk the Shocker, and cousin to the talented singer/rapper Mo B. Dick.

At just five years old, Lil Romeo’s journey in the music world began when he was signed to Soulja Music Entertainment, a subsidiary label of No Limit Records. His father, Master P, recognized his potential and talent early on, and this would mark the beginning of a legendary career.

Lil Romeo’s Career Milestones

In 2001, Lil Romeo’s debut album, “Lil’ Romeo,” was released under No Limit Records, and it instantly made waves in the music industry. The album featured the chart-topping single “My Baby,” which helped

it secure the sixth position on the Billboard 200 and the fifth position on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart. It also achieved Gold certification from the RIAA, having sold over 500,000 copies in the United States.

The following year, in 2002, Lil Romeo released his second studio album, “Game Time,” which solidified his status as a musical sensation. Though it didn’t reach the same chart positions as his debut album, it was a commercial success, selling over 2.5 million copies in the US. His third studio album, “Romeoland,” further contributed to his rising fame.

Lil Romeo’s Personal Life

Despite his early success and fame, Lil Romeo faced unique challenges as a child star. The pressure to excel in a highly competitive industry while dealing with bullying and the lack of understanding from others made his journey even more commendable. However, he persevered and continued to shine both academically and athletically.

Lil Romeo attended Beverly Hills High School in California, and he later received a full athletic scholarship from the University of Southern California to play basketball. Beyond his professional accomplishments, he also experienced personal joy, welcoming his first child, a baby girl, with his girlfriend, Drew Sangster, on February 14, 2022.
