Grace Charis' chest dubbed 'poetry in motion' as she runs across golf course

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Grace Charis' little run across a golf course left her fans all saying the same thing on social media.

The Californian native has quickly establisher herself as one of the most popular golf influencers in the country. Charis is edging towards two million followers on Instagram, despite only taking up golf in 2021.

While she has never come close to the women's professional circuit, Charis is a huge star in the niche that is influencing golfing. After she ran across the golf course wearing a snug tank top, it became clear as to why fans enjoyed her content so much.
