All the times Taylor Swift hinted at her break-up with Joe Alwyn

Publish date: 2024-07-14

It was exclusively revealed by DailyMail.com that Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn's romance began to struggle over their career differences before their recent split.

Insiders claimed that the global superstar's career overshadowed her beau's once the pandemic ended, as the actor 'grappled to become leading man material'.

The singer, 33 and the Brit, 32, shocked fans with the revelation they had gone their separate ways after six years last week - with a source saying the issue drove a wedge between the pair, especially as Taylor headed back on tour.

Taylor has thrown herself back into her career of late, releasing acclaimed new album Midnights and heading back on the road for The Eras Tour  

ET claimed last week that the Lavender Haze songstress - who is currently on tour - and the UK actor allegedly had an amicable split that was 'not dramatic.'

Ever since news of their shock split broke, Taylor's fans have been looking for clues in her songs and performances that she had broken up with Joe.

The American pop star has become known for dropping cryptic messages known as 'easter eggs' into her tracks.

Taylor loves leaving a trail of breadcrumbs in her music, in 2019, she said of her fans: 'I’ve trained them to be that way, I love that they like the cryptic hint-dropping. Because as long as they like it, I’ll keep doing it.

'It’s fun. It feels mischievous and playful.'

Switching up her set list 

Fans believe that one of the first signs Taylor and Joe had gone their separate ways was when she switched out one song for another during a recent gig.

Swifties believe she hinted at her split from Joe more than a week ago with a surprise addition to the setlist on her Eras tour.

After news of the pair's split broke on April 9, fans took to Twitter to point out that Swift played performed song 'The 1' for the first time ever on March 31. 

Fans speculated that her performance of the song - in which a narrator laments about breaking up with a possible soulmate - was Swift's way of hinting she had split from Alwyn. The song replaced 'Invisible String' on the setlist - a song about soulmates being drawn together by fate.

When her album 'Folklore' was released in 2020, many speculated that 'Invisible String' was packed with clues suggesting it was about her and Alwyn's relationship.

When it was swapped out for 'The 1' in March - noting before the performance, 'You know, I've never played that song live before' - fans didn't bat an eye. 

But they later took Twitter to speculate that the performance was proof that rumors were real once the news of the split had been reveald.
