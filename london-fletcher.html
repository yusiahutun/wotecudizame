London Levi Fletcher-Baker (born May 19, 1975) is a former American football linebacker who played in the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at John Carroll, and signed with the St. Louis Rams as an undrafted free agent in 1998. Fletcher also played for the Buffalo Bills and Washington Redskins. He made four Pro Bowls in his career, and was also a part of the Rams' victory in Super Bowl XXXIV.
