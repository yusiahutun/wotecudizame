Emma Watkins confirms huge fan theory about The Wiggles

Publish date: 2024-08-26

After years of speculation, Emma Watkins has finally addressed a fan theory about her character in The Wiggles.

The 30-year-old children's entertainer confirmed to Allure what many parents had suspected for years: she wears a wig while performing.

Emma explained that she switched to a wig in 2013, a year after joining the band.

Mystery solved! Emma Watkins (second from left) has confirmed what many parents had suspected for years: she wears a wig while performing. Pictured with her co-stars (left to right) Simon Pryce, Lachlan Gillespie and Anthony Field on November 27 in Sydney

'I'm about to confirm that I do wear a wig in The Wiggles show! There's been lots of chat on all different forums about whether it is my real hair or not,' she began.

'Initially at the beginning [in 2012], I was using my own hair and that's why it was quite confusing. Especially when people were watching my first season, compared to the second one. 

'So yes, I do wear a wig and I don't just have one, I have about four wigs now. Some for touring, some for filming overseas, [and] one at home all the time, just in case.'

Spot the difference! Emma used her natural hair during her first season with The Wiggles in 2012 (left), but switched to a wig the following year (right)

Emma decided to start wearing a wig because she found herself struggling to recreate her character's distinctive look for each performance. 

She said: 'After about a year and a half, my hair started to fall out because I was parting it in the same place everyday. 

'That's why I invested in a good wig. It is so much easier, and it gives a sense of consistency to the character, Emma Wiggle.'
