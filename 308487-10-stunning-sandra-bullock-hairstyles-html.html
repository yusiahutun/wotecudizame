10 stunning Sandra Bullock hairstyles 2020

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Sandra Bullock is one of the most beautiful and loved Hollywood celebrities of our generation. This is given to her down to earth nature, professional acting skills, and not forgetting her trendy taste in fashion and beauty. Sandra Bullock hairstyles prove that beauty resides in simplicity, consistency, and acceptance of change. Sandra Bullock is still making great trends for her fans.

The Oscar award-winning actress is known for her seasonal fashionista styles that are easily achievable by many women who wish to explore new fantastic looks. As a result, we present to you the latest collection of Sandra Bullock hairstyles to help you make the right hairstyle choice this summer. Take a look.

1. Blunt Bob - Sandra Bullock hairstyles in Bird Box

As the year came to an end, Sandra decided that it was time to switch from her trademark long hairstyle hence cutting it to a short blunt bob. This became Sandra Bullock new haircut. Bullock made the cut based on her role in the Netflix film “Bird Box.” According to her interview with Hoda Kotb, the actress said, "When I did cut it, I went, 'Oh, OK, that's me. I can be this person now.' It's all about the hair." She added, "It's funny. I feel like my hair was there to help me hide. I hide behind my hair."

2. Formal bob (medium straight )

Bullock cut her long hair on the edges hence achieving a long bob look that marks one of the latest Sandra Bullock hairstyles. The bob cut is made around the head and heights aligned to the jaw and ear level. Wearing the formal-straight bob results to an appealing focus of her oval-shaped face. The final blunt cuts on the edges are necessary to add that natural touch. The top hair is dark with lighter tips hence that contrast effect that makes her hair beautiful.

To achieve this look, ensure that you cut your hair to a medium height with blunt edges. The formal bob wear best suits women with oval faces and medium hair density and texture. Achieving the cut will take you an average of 20 minutes and requires a straightening iron, spray, and other straightening product for that perfect look.

3. Long straight bangs

The actress uses a dark Mocha hair color for that fantastic brunette effect. The full bangs bring all the focus on her well-done eyes and cheekbones. Thanks to her straight hair texture, the cut comes out natural while the long strands reaching her neck and shoulder are trimmed to shape her squared jawline to an oval facial appearance.

The cut should be focused on long strand layers located on the front side of your temple and both sides of your eyelashes. This should be followed by blowing perfect waves on the hair until you achieve that smooth feel alignment. Ensure that your bangs are full to frame your temple at levels reaching the eyebrows.

Long straight bangs best suit women with oval faces complimenting their well-structured cheekbone appearance. You can achieve the casual look by using a pair of scissors, a blow dryer, and products such as smoothing shine, your spray of choice, and a moisturizer.

4. Long wavy (formal)

As one of the stunning looks ever to be pulled by Bullock, the beauty placed her long locks over her front shoulder with the lower to middle lengths possessing curly waves. Hot roller equipment is used to achieve great waves. You will need a scissor cut to trim protruding strands that do not align with the smooth waves. The style best suits formal occasions. Some of the equipment’s range from curling iron/hot rollers and a blow dryer. Product to use include spray, wax, smoothing shine, and mousse.

5. Long curly up-do

Bullock wore this style during the 2006 Oscar Awards event. To achieve the unique and beautiful look, the Oscar winner had both her top hair and side hair pinned at the high-top part of the head. She also had her back hair forming waves.This is achieved by twisting hence producing messy waves. Furthermore, the front section includes well done curly hair that makes the Hollywood star’s face become the center of attraction. Long curly up-do is an all-weather look that requires a curling iron, mousse, among other hair creams.

6. Medium straight

The actress stole the limelight by wearing the medium straight casual style in the MTV Movie Awards that were held in 2006. Wearing the style will require you to make blunt edge cuts in what happens to resemble a blunt bob.

7. Medium straight bob

Sandra wore the brunette casual straight bob in July 2009 during the official launching of “The Lake House.” The hairstyle is best for simple but classy and decent looks such as dates. By using a scissor cut, let the length reach the shoulder level. To achieve the smooth hair lining, cut off any protruding hair strands that result of a streamlined round face appeal.

8. Pixie cut

Sandra Bullock was featured as a 2013 Vogue Magazine cover celebrity where she wore a gorgeous pixie haircut. At the back of her short brunette hair, there were short wips that brought the best of her jawline, eyes, and correctly done eyebrows.

9. Long wide swept bangs

The side swept style draws attention to Sandra’s gorgeous body, shoulder, and chick bone. Wearing the style requires you to make waves on the middle to the lower sides and swiping the bangs on either side of your eyebrows such that they cover almost your entire right or left eyebrows.

10. Blonde, long, and straight

In 2014, Bullock decided to go blond as a preparation to participate in the film production of the comedy film Our Brand Is Crisis. She has pulled this look in movies such as The Blind Side (2009) as Leigh Anne Tuhoy, a part that led to her achievement of winning an Oscar award.

This is one of the simplest and common looks that make part of Sandra Bullock’s hairstyles over the years. The free-falling loose and long hair is cut at the end to increase the texture hence bringing it to life.
