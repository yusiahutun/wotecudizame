Celebrity Hairstylist Sally Hershberger's New Salon

Publish date: 2024-06-03

What: Sally Hershberger | Tim Rogers Salon in the NoMad district in Manhattan.

Who: Hairstylists Sally Hershberger and Tim Rogers, plus the core squad of stylists and colorists from Sally Hershberger Downtown Salon that closed this summer.

The celebrity clients: Meg Ryan, John Mayer, Jimmy Fallon, Tom Cruise, Olivia Newton John, Joan Jett

The space: It's a 5,000 square foot expanse on the second floor of a prewar-style walk-up, outfitted with 28 chairs. The duo tapped architect Paul Bennett,who has created similarly industrial luxe spaces for Balenciaga, Ralph Lauren, and Calvin Klein. Bennett opted for charcoal wood floors, stainless steel fixtures, and accentuated a bank of south-facing windows, which flood the salon with natural light, similar to the former Meatpacking location. Another thoughtful touch? All blowdryer cords will come from the ceiling to avoid unseemly tripping.

Paul Bennett Architects, LLP

The partnership: "When my lease was up at Sally Hershberger Downtown Salon, I called Tim, who'd been a stylist there for years. I couldn't think of anybody that would be more suited to my personality. And Tim said yes right away, which is exactly how I work. Tim is so professional with the day-ins and -outs of a salon, whereas I'm out and about a lot," says Hershberger.

Sally Hershberger | Tim Rogers Salon

Sally Hershberger and Tim Rogers.

Hidden treasures: Hershberger's personal art collection is on full display, including an Andy Warhol silkscreen of Mick Jagger, plus original photography from Bert Stern, Annie Leibovitz, Herb Ritts, Steven Meisel, and Patrick Demarchelier.

The playlist: Burt Bacharach, Dionne Warwick, Rihanna and Drake. "We keep it sexy in the morning, then break into the disco around 2, because we need a pick up," says Hershberger.

The cuts: "My signature hair is sexy messy-chic with a cool downtown edge," says Hershberger.

The extras: Also operating out of the space is the skincare clinic The Face Place, which will provide facials. The salon will also offer manicures, pedicures, and a proprietary placental-stem-cell-derived growth factor treatment formulated by Dr. Roberta Shapiro targeted at kickstarting dormant hair follicles.

The products: Hershberger's professional hair care line, 24K.

The prices: Cuts begin at $150+; color $150+; highlights $250+, extensions $1000+

Sally Hershberger | Tim Rogers Salon, 25 West 26th Street, 2nd Floor, sallyhershberger.com.
