Jessica Biel Posts Rare Photo with Son Silas as She Reveals Their Morning Routine

Publish date: 2024-04-25

Jessica Biel is loving life as a mom to 10-month-old Silas.

The actress took to Instagram to give fans a rare glimpse of her son with Justin Timberlake. While we can't see the little guy's face, it's clear she has her hands full with this tiny tot.

"[Three things I can't live without are] yoga, eating breakfast with my kid [and] listening to Erykah Badu while I eat with my kid. We love her," Biel writes in Q&A segment with The Skimm.

Mornings start early for the famous family, and Jessica admits she gets up right away when the alarm goes off. "[I get up] two hours before I'd really like to," she shares. "I'm not a snoozer. Never have been. Wish I was, but I would never get up if I did."

One thing she can't live without? "Bullet Proof Decaf Coffee."

The 33-year-old star also refers to herself as "Mama Bear, actress, producer, wannabe alpinist, consummate student of the @WomanCareGlobal #ThenWhoWill campaign."

Even though she's a working mom with a lot on her plate, she loves every minute of it. "[I woke up] disheveled, running late, doing too many things at once and underperforming all of them; but pretty thrilled about mediocrity! Could be a lot worse, guys. A lot worse," she writes.

Having JT helping out at home has its perks too, right?!
