Horror as barefoot man is found hanged inside subway tunnel under New York City's Grand Central Stat

Publish date: 2024-06-16

A barefoot man has been found hanged inside a subway tunnel near Manhattan's Grand Central Station.

Officers said the unidentified man was discovered hanging from his neck by a wire, around 100ft down the tunnel of the No. 7 train. 

He was found by MTA workers at around 5:15am, and was said to be unconscious and unresponsive before he was pronounced dead at the scene. 

A man was found hanged inside a subway tunnel near Manhattan's Grand Central Station (pictured) in the early hours of Saturday morning

Officials noted that no criminality is suspected over the incident, and it appears to be a suicide. 

Horrific footage purported to be of the man's body was shared to social media, attracting a large number of views. 

It alleged to show the man barefoot but fully clothed hanging in the tunnel, with NYPD Emergency Service United officers readying to put him in a body bag.
