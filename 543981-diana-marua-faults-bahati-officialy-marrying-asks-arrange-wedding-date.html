Diana Marua Faults Bahati for Not Officially Marrying Her, Asks Him to Arrange Wedding Date

Publish date: 2024-02-15
  • Diana Marua and her hubby Bahati got into a discussion about when they intend to hold their official white wedding
  • Bahati defended himself and revealed he gave Diana potential working dates, but she turned them down for various reasons
  • The YouTuber asked her husband to show he is serious by planning a wedding date proposal so she can decide if she is okay with it or not

Content creator Diana Marua has put her husband Bahati on the spot, demanding to know when he intends to wed her officially.

In their new YouTube video, Diana faulted Bahati for not marrying her months after proposing to her and later holding their ruracio.

When Bahati intends to marry Diana Marua

However, in his defence, Bahati asked Diana when she would like to get married and reminded her that he had proposed dates, but she turned them down.

"I do not want issues; I already set the date on December 12, and she said a lot of things are happening on that day and people are partying. I shared we do it on April 1, and you said it looked like it's an April Fools Day, which will make my marriage look like a lie. Can I marry you on October 20 on our anniversary date?" he said.

Diana then requested that Bahati arrange a proper wedding date proposal and take her out to discuss their plans.

"A wedding is something well-thought about. Even your approach about it to me should be very genuine and from the heart. If its an announcement, you make it special, you take me out for dinner, make sure I am well dressed. Do like a proposal date like 'honey I would like to marry you on this date, do you agree?' then I say yes or no," she said.
