The glitter-packed limited edition body scrub you have to try

Publish date: 2024-03-29

    Australian beauty label Frank Body have launched an ultra-sparkly body scrub that bestows an otherworldly glistening glow upon your skin. While sloughing away dead skin cells and delivering a serious dose of hydration, the must-have scrub’s microscopic gold and silver particles hug your skin, leaving you looking radiant and utterly ravishing.

    The brand new Shimmer Scrub is a beauty must-try

    Presented in an Instagram-able holographic pouch, the limited edition scrub will have you looking disco-ready in an instant. The idea is to take the body buffer into the bath or shower, rub it into your skin, rinse, et voilà… Baby-soft, subtly shimmering limbs are all yours.

    The more of the exfoliator you use, the higher the concentration of glitter left on your skin and even better, it doesn’t transfer onto your clothes. Packed with deliciously-scented ground coffee beans and nourishing grapeseed oil, the sweet-smelling body polish is just the thing for prepping your body for balmier weather. 

    Sure to leave you disco-ready, the new body polish is packed with tiny gold and silver sparkle particles

    It caused a social media frenzy in the days before its launch on 1st May and garnered a 50,000 person waiting list. The scrub is set to sell out before the end of the week but you can still get your hands on it at uk.frankbody.com.

    View post on Instagram 

    Frank Body’s antioxidant-rich coffee scrubs have become a cult favourite over the course of the past couple of years. Not only is coffee a brilliant natural exfoliator, it has the same pH as your skin and so it won’t strip it of moisture or leave behind a nasty, oily residue. Other must-try products from the brand include the skin-smoothing Coconut Body Balm and the oh-so-addictive Lip Scrub and Lip Balm Duo.
