Brett Eldredge Biography

Brett Eldredge is best known as Singer-songwriter who has an estimated Net Worth of $5 Million. Country singer whose 2010 debut single “Raymond” reached #52 on the country music charts and his song “Don't Ya” reached #1. His 2013 album Bring You Back was a #2 hit. At the 2014 CMA Awards, he was named New Artist of the Year. He was born on March 23,1986, Paris, IL. He had his first performance at the Grand Ole Opry in 2010. He studied at Chicago's Elmhurst College. He co-wrote the song “I Think I've Had Enough” for Gary Allan. His cousin is Terry Eldredge of The Grascals. He has a brother named Brice. His zodiac sign is Aries.
