Floral Street Sunshine Bloom Scented Candle

1

Floral Street Sunshine Bloom Scented Candle

Spring is always a little deceiving because it can still feel an awful lot like winter. But not when you have this candle burning. Inspired by sunlight, Sunshine Bloom has notes of jasmine petals, fig leaves, incense, wild orchid, cashmere wood, and coconut to conjure up a warm and sunny day.

TOM FORD Lost Cherry Candle

You wouldn't expect a candle with cherry in the name to be warm and spicy (or maybe you would?), but either way, this is the perfect scent to burn on a romantic Spring night.

Birthdate Candles Birthdate Candles

5

Birthdate Candles Birthdate Candles

Now 25% Off

Combining astrology, tarot, and numerology, a Birthdate Candle is exactly what it sounds like. Astrologers consult on what elements, flowers, and oils are used for each astrological sign’s custom scent, and each jar comes with fun facts about your birth chart and personality on the label. If nothing else, this makes for the perfect gift.

Fornasetti Medium Rossetti Scented Candle

7

Fornasetti Medium Rossetti Scented Candle

Credit: Fornasetti

A Fornasetti candle isn’t just a candle—it’s a work of art. These painted lips will instantly make any room feel cooler, but we really love this on a makeup vanity. The floral and sophisticated notes of lily of the valley, iris, and tuberose are so lovely.

8

Glory Skincare Cozy Up & Groove Candles Set

8

Glory Skincare Cozy Up & Groove Candles Set

Credit: Glory Skincare

Are you in the mood for something warm? Or do you want something a little bit more energizing? This set from Glory Skincare makes it so you don’t have to choose. The Cozy Up candle has notes of bergamot and white musk to help you chill while Groove has notes of eucalyptus and mint to instantly liven up any room.

Gilded Pink Onyx Marble Candle

Le Labo Santal 26 Classic Candle

14

Byredo Cactus Jack for BYREDO - Travx Candle

14

Byredo Cactus Jack for BYREDO - Travx Candle

Credit: Byredo

A Travis Scott and Byredo collab was the partnership we didn’t know we needed last year. Both the fragrance and candle sold out immediately but now they're back in stock. Straying away from Byredo’s minimal aesthetic in this cool blue and purple ombré jar, this candle smells like space. No, really: It contains notes of cosmic dust, starlight, and supernova. It’s surprising, weird, and works together in the most wondrous ways.
