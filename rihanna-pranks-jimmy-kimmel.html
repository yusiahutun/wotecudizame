Rihanna Pranks Jimmy Kimmel

Publish date: 2024-02-15

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — With a little help from Jimmy Kimmel's wife, Molly McKneary, Rihanna and some cohorts pulled an April Fools Day prank on Kimmel by sneaking into his bedroom while he was sleeping at night and staging an impromptu concert.

The prank, recorded on video, shows Rihanna and several associates surrounding Kimmel's bed and then waking him up with colored flashing lights to the tune of Rihanna's "Bitch Better Have My Money."

Rihanna appeared on "Jimmy Kimmel Live!" that night, where they discussed the prank, as well as Rihanna's penchant for pranking people on her tour with gems such as the old "hidden raw fish in the luggage" gag.

"I love playing pranks on people," Rihanna said. "My band: they've suffer the most … because on the road you get a little bored, and you get into a little mischief, especially on the last shows …"

After hearing this, Kimmel admitted that he felt that perhaps he got off a little easy with just being woken up. – Staff Writers
