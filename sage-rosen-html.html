Sage Rosen - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Professional dancer who competed in Nickelodeon's Lip Sync Battle Shorties and later became a pro dancer on Dancing with the Stars: Juniors. He has danced in music videos for stars like Justin Bieber, Chris Brown and Sia. He has also performed with Kidz Bop and has competed nationally. 

Before Fame

He signed with Bloc LA at a young age. One of his early successes as a dancer was in 2015 performing in an episode of Nickelodeon's Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn.  

Trivia

In 2017, he became a Radix Teen Male Core Performer and has performed as a member of ImmaBeast. 

Family Life

His middle name is Asher. Born into a family of eight, he shares the YouTube channel Rosen Fam with his parents and siblings. Together they create vlogs, challenges, and prank videos. 

Associated With

He joined Maddie Ziegler as a dancer in Sia's "The Greatest" music video in 2016.
