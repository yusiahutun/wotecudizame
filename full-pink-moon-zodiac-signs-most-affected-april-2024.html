4 Zodiac Signs Most Affected

Publish date: 2024-10-09

If you’re feeling exhausted and confused, you’re not alone. You’ve survived a tumultuous eclipse season and you’re still dealing with the erratic changes of a Jupiter-Uranus conjunction. And if you’re one of the zodiac signs affected by the full Pink Moon in Scorpio of April 2024 the most, major developments are still unfolding in your life.

Every 28 days, a full moon brings climactic changes that alter your perception and urge you to take action. This is when the moon—ruler of your internal self—forms an opposition with the sun—ruler of your external self—causing deep-seated truths to be flung to the surface. A full moon excavates hidden gems from the bottom floor of your psyche, showing you all that you’ve always known, but simply have forgotten. It gets to the nitty gritty of what you’re really feeling and forces you to do something about it. You can’t ignore what is impossible to repress.

On April 23, a full Pink Moon in Scorpio will reach its peak at exactly 7:49 p.m. ET. Scorpio—the fixed water sign of merging energies, emotional intimacy, financial investments, strategic advancements, taboo truths, transformations, and metamorphosis—is urging you to be vulnerable without completely dropping your defenses. It’s time to learn how to swim without sinking. Opposing the sun in grounded, sensual, romantic, practical, and patient Taurus, this full moon wants you to find balance between what belongs to you and what you’re willing to give to someone else. At its core, the Taurus-Scorpio axis is about your material needs and your spiritual needs. It’s about what you keep to yourself and the secrets you choose to share. While Taurus wants to maintain their own money, Scorpio is more concerned with investing their money. And what Taurus wants to keep, Scorpio just wants to give and receive.

Rising at 4 degrees of Scorpio, the full Pink Moon in Scorpio is bound to bring up uncomfortable truths. Scorpio is a sign that can see in the dark, and even when the facts aren’t being presented, this fixed water sign can always see what others are not seeing. This full moon will also square off with Pluto in Aquarius, and because Pluto is the planet of creation and destruction, it may encourage you to tear something down for the sake of rebuilding it with more strength, authenticity, and reinforcement. This full moon is sparking an internal revolution, so lead yourself to the victory you have always forseen. Which of the 12 astrological houses are ruled by Taurus and Scorpio in your birth chart? These areas of your life shine a revealing light on how this full moon will affect you.

These 4 Zodiac Signs Will Feel the Full Moon the Most

If your sun sign, moon sign or—most importantly—your rising sign falls under any of the following zodiac signs (especially if lands between 0 and 10 degrees), the upcoming full Pink Moon in Scorpio could lead to a spiritual revolution. Here’s why

  • Taurus

    You are not the same person that you were before, Taurus. A Jupiter-Uranus conjunction in Taurus guarantees that much. This alignment took place on April 20 and it’s still shaking up your life and your sense of identity. You’re embracing a side of yourself that may feel completely foreign to you. And as much as you may want a certain relationship to work out, not everyone can come with you as you embark on this next phase of life. As this full moon activates your seventh house of partnerships, you may become aware of someone else’s actions and intentions (and whether they truly have your best interest at heart). Your one-on-one relationships are being illuminated by this full moon, showing you who your allies and enemies really are. It will also bring your awareness to how you can attract relationships that align with your newfound values. If conflict has been burning up, this full moon could highlight tensions, giving you a chance to remedy the situation, communicate with each other, and work on finding a solution together. Get ready for the world to become aware of the strides you’ve been making, especially as it pertains to your biggest goals. With a full Pink Moon rising in your first house of the self, you’re the main character of this story and you have the power to be your own hero rather than wait for someone to save you. Your relationships have been unpredictable and you may be discovering that you could never rely on certain people. And as this full moon squares off with Pluto in your fourth house of home and family, you may need to overcome certain patterns from your childhood in order to set yourself free from what has been holding you back.
