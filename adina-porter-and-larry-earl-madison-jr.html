About

Adina Porter is a 52 year old American Actress. Born Adina Elizabeth Porter on 13th March, 1971 in New York City, New York, USA, she is famous for her roles as Lettie Mae Thornton in the HBO dark fantasy horror series True Blood, Kendra James in the HBO drama series The Newsroom, Indra in the CW science fiction drama series The 100 and Sally Freeman, Lee Harris, Beverly Hope, Dinah Stevens, and Chief Burleson in the FX anthology horror series American Horror Story in a career that spans 1988–present.
