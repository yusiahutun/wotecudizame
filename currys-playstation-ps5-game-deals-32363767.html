Get a PlayStation 5 Slim and two free games in eye-catching Currys deal

Publish date: 2024-04-18

Gamers are in for a treat this month thanks to some awesome PlayStation 5 deals from Currys. The retailer has slashed prices on a range of console and game bundles, including on the newly released PS5 Slim.

Sony launched the ‘Slim’ model of its latest console in November, three years since the release of its larger sibling. As the name suggests, the Slim is a more compact version of the standard PS5, coming in 24 percent lighter and 30 percent smaller.

It also boasts 1TB of storage compared to the previous model’s 825GB and a second USB-C port, along with 8K graphics and HDR technology. Shoppers can currently pick up a Slim PS5 from Currys with a cool £70 discount – now just £409 for the console and a DualSense Wireless Controller – but there’s also some awesome bundles that come with up to four games.

Gamers can buy the PS5 Slim with LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga and Wreckfest for £429, which works out at just £10 per game. With this, Star Wars and LEGO fans alike can explore a galaxy far, far away, or indulge in some demolition derby action with Wreckfest.

If football is more your thing, there’s the PS5 Slim with EA Sports FC 24 and Wreckfest for £439. But if two games just isn’t enough, Currys offers a packed PS5 Slim and four-game bundle for £519 that comes with Spider-Man 2, Hogwarts Legacy, LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga and Wreckfest.

If you want to keep costs even further down and are happy to stick with the standard PS5, there’s some equally great deals over at GAME, like the standard PS5 and EA Sports FC 24 for just £409. Shoppers can also swap the PS5 for the Slim model for an extra £20, while Potterheads can explore the Wizarding World with the PS5 Slim and Hogwarts Legacy bundle for £429.

Currys customers can get free next day delivery on selected items and up to £250 off when trading in an old console. Currys also offers up to three months of free Apple Music, Apple TV+, Apple Arcade and Apple Fitness+ with these PS5 bundles.
