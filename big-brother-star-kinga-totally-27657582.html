Big Brother star Kinga totally unrecognisable 17 years on from wild wine bottle stint

Publish date: 2024-04-30

Since appearing on Big Brother 17 years ago, reality TV star Kinga Karolczak now looks totally different.

The TV personality first shot to fame on the 2005 series of Big Brother and hit the headlines after getting up close and personal a bottle of wine.

The brazen star entered the Big Brother house halfway through the programme's series six and caused quite a stir with her racy actions.

Kinga allegedly pleasured herself with a wine bottle in the Big Brother garden in front of her housemates.

The X-rated scene went down as one of TV’s most shocking moments.

Speaking of the incident in 2012, Kinga said: "On Big Brother they want you to go crazy so give you loads of alcohol. And drinking makes me behave like a completely different person.

"So, I just put the bottle between my legs, ran into the garden and did it again. I know I didn't even lift up my nightie. I didn't have sex in the pool or in the house, but everyone's labelling me as the slapper."

Her wild persona also meant that she often clashed with her co-stars which made for TV gold at the time.

She ended up finishing in a very respectable fourth place and continued to try to stay in the public eye after that.

With a taste for fame, she also featured in a low-budget film called Cash and Curry alongside Lee Latchford-Evans from Steps.

In October 2008, she made a guest appearance on Charlie Brooker's E4 horror series Dead Set in a cameo as herself as zombies entered the Big Brother house.

She has also starred on other TV shows such as Big Brother’s Little Brother, Big Brother’s Big Mouth, The Charlotte Church Show and The Sunday Night Project.

In recent years, she has ditched her iconic blonde Big Brother look and gone for brunette locks instead.

According to reports, she has lost over three stone and has been busy focusing on her YouTube channel called The Kinga Show.

On her channel, Kinga kept her viewers updated about her weight loss, makeup and fashion purchases.

Kinga wrote: ”I am not being paid by any of the mentioned companies or designers to make these videos.

“The views in my videos are strictly my own and I am not affiliated with any of these companies.”
