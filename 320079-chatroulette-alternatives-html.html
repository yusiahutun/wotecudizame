Chatroulette is an online chat website that randomly pairs users to engage in webcam conversations. Its premise is simple. A user begins an online chat in the form of video, audio, or text with a different user. At some point in their conversation, either person may decide to leave the chat and initiate a different one with another random person.

Earlier on, the site was one of the friendliest places on the internet to meet people from across the globe. But over the years, its status has declined, and only a few users visit it. That has prompted many people to look for Chatroulette alternatives to start communicating with strangers.

Random online video chat is the new trend today. The online community seems to enjoy it because one does not have to be specific on the type of people they want to communicate with. If you are bored at home, you can log in to a chat room and initiate a conversation with as many random people as possible.
