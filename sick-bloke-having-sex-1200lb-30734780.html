Sick bloke having sex with a 1,200lb horse finally caught in act by 'secret camera'

Publish date: 2024-07-19

A sick bloke has finally admitted to having sex with a horse two years after being caught on camera doing so.

Jackson Kelley, 21, pleaded guilty last Friday to animal cruelty after appearing at the Norfolk Super Court in Massachusetts, United States.

He was caught on a secret camera taking part in the horrific act at the Turner Hill Equestrian Stable sat around 4.30am on July 15, 2021.

He had hoped to cover his tracks by unplugging all the cameras in the stables, but appeared to have missed one – which captured everything.

He was also seen exiting the stables while pulling up his pants.

In very graphic detail, the court heard how he assaulted the horse 10 times across 38 minutes, using a step ladder to help him with the crime.

Superior Court Judge Beverly Cannone handed down a sentence of four years supervised probation and ordered him to complete a period of psychiatric treatment for sexually paraphiliac behaviour.

Judge Cannone also ordered Kelley to have his medication assessed and possibly adjusted; to not drink or use drugs; and to have no contact with anyone involved in the case, or with animals, including working with them or volunteering with organisations that work with animals.

He also faced a second charge, one of sexual conduct with an animal.

Turner Hill’s owner, Hillary Swarr, said at the time that the horse was checked by a veterinarian and was found to be uninjured.
She also explained that a stable employee was alerted remotely by an alarm triggered by the stall door being opened.

The live feed was then checked but because of the physical distances involved, the pervert was gone before anyone arrived.
After his picture was sent to neighbouring police forces, Kelley turned himself in several days later.

He is now awaiting sentencing.
