Victoria's Secret angel Behati dazzles in new Fabulous Bra

Publish date: 2024-04-02

    Victoria's Secret angel Behati Prinsloo has had fans coveting her toned abs as she stars in the new campaign for the relaunch of the lingerie giant's world-famous Fabulous Bra. The 25-year-old poses in the bra and skinny jeans, looking flawless as she goes make-up free and wears her hair down in tousled waves.

    Victoria's Secret angel Behati Prinsloo stars in the new Fabulous Bra campaign

    The Namibian model, who is married to Maroon 5 lead singer Adam Levine, proudly gave fans a sneak peek of the campaign on her Instagram account as the lingerie hit stores in the UK, sharing a photo in which she shows off her sultry side for the camera. "New @victoriassecret fabulous collection, go buy your bestie a bra," she captioned the image. The young model also stars in a behind-the-scenes video of the photoshoot, beaming as she talks about being in New York. The gorgeous bra comes in a variety of colours including blue, pink and grey, with the brand launching fragrance Fabulous alongside the new lingerie.

    Behati has been an angel since 2009

    It's been a busy few months for Behati, who has been an angel since 2009. Not only did she open the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show held in London last December, but she has since gone on to star in the brand's Super Bowl advert earlier this year, as well as joining nine other models to star in the brand's annual Swim catalogue. Talking exclusively to HELLO! Fashion backstage at the show, Behati revealed that she had been extremely nervous about being the first to take to the catwalk. "It's so amazing and so stressful all at the same time," she said. "It's such a huge moment that I will always be nervous." Earlier this year, fellow angels Karlie Kloss and Doutzen Kroes shocked the industry after it was revealed just days apart that they will be leaving Victoria's Secret to pursue other projects. However, there are clearly no hard feelings, with the brand's President and chief marketing officer Ed Razek describing Karlie as 'one of the best models ever' and Doutzen as 'one of the most stunningly beautiful people in the world'.
