Who is Desi Arnaz Jr?

Desiderio Alberto Arnaz IV, alias Desi Arnaz Jr, is an actor and musician. He gained fame before birth because his parents were in the limelight.

He gained fame when he was still in his mother's womb. His mother starred in I Love Lucy when she was pregnant with him. Her pregnancy was part of the sitcom's storyline.

Who are Desi Arnaz Jr's parents?

The musician's mother is the late Lucille Désirée Ball, while his father is the late Desiderio Alberto Arnaz y de Acha III (Desi Arnaz). He has a sister named Lucie Arnaz. Lucie is an actress who was born in 1951.

From 2002 to 2007, the actor was vice-president of the board of Directors of the Lucille Ball–Desi Arnaz Centre in Jamestown, New York. He resigned because of a dispute with the executive director.

Boulder Theatre

In 1997, the actor bought the Boulder Theatre when it was on the brink of ruin. He renovated the cinema and made it the home of the Boulder City Ballet Company.

He owns the Historic Boulder Theatre and helps direct the non-profit Boulder City Ballet Company (BCBC).

Ricci, Desi, & Billy

In 1998, the actor went back to music. He toured with a new configuration of Dino, Desi & Billy called Ricci, Desi, & Billy. The group performed original material as well as other songs.

Relationships and marriages

It is known that romantic relationships contributed to the actor's tragic life. Desi Arnaz Jr started dating at a young age. He once said he was under the illusion of love at 13 and added that he was a playboy.

At 15, he became a father. He was in a relationship with model Susan Callahan-Howe in 1968. The two were 15 when they became parents to Julia Arnaz. Julia's paternity was proven in 1991.

At 17 and 23, he dated actress Patty Duke. He accompanied Duke to the 1970 Emmy Awards ceremony, where she won the Emmy for Outstanding Single Performance.

Lucille Ball, his mother, did not approve of her son's relationship with Duke. Duke became pregnant, and the two broke up.

After parting ways, Michael Tell, a writer and music producer, offered to Marry Duke. Their marriage lasted 13 days.

What happened to Desi Arnaz Jr?

From the moment he was born, the actor and musician was in the public eye. In 2015, he decided to step away from the limelight.

He relocated from Los Angeles, California, to Boulder City, Nevada, because he felt the urge to start a new and different life.

He said the move was driven by the need to know himself better. All his life, he was in the media because of his famous parents and not knowing who he was. He was trapped in a system that excluded reality.
