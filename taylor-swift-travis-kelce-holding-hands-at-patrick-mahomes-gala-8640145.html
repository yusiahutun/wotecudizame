Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce at Patrick Mahomes' Gala

Publish date: 2024-06-23

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have his-and-hers street style down to an art by now, but on their latest outing, they tried out black tie couples dressing for the first time. And spoiler alert: they nailed it. While stepping out together to support Patrick Mahomes’ charity gala in Las Vegas on Saturday, April 27, the “Karma” singer and the Kansas City Chiefs star looked like they were ready for their red carpet debut.

In videos and photos from attendees shared on Instagram and X (formerly Twitter), Swift could be seen holding hands with Kelce while wearing a strapless olive green dress with a structured bodice and a back cutout design. The singer, who’s fresh off the release of her latest album The Tortured Poets Department, paired her signature red lip with natural waves, black winged eyeliner, and a Y2K-esque chain link choker necklace with a diamond charm. Meanwhile, Kelce leaned into the leading man aesthetic with a navy blue suit paired with an unbuttoned white dress shirt.

During the 15 and Mahomies Foundation Golf Classic gala, the athlete gave Swift a sweet shout-out while revealing that she’d decided to give away four surprise Eras Tour tickets for auction. According to Auctioneer Harry Santa’s Instagram, the tickets ended up going for $80,000.

“I was just talking to my significant other and uh,” Kelce said, pausing for effect. “We might have one other auction item that wasn’t on the docket. Has anyone heard of the Eras Tour?”

Only time will tell whether Swelce makes their first red carpet appearance at this year’s Met Gala, but judging from their coordinating looks at this event, we're getting excited for this couple's black tie era.
