Emily Ratajkowski's Fur-Lined Leather Trench Coat Is the Perfect Fall Staple

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Emily Ratajkowski’s fall outfit formula includes two perfect leather staples.

The model stepped out in New York City on Halloween in a sleek all-black fit—her sexy take on spooky. The outfit consisted only of a great coat and great boots, which, sometimes, is all you really need.

The coat was a chic leather trench with dramatic fur-lined trim at the collar and sleeves. (A similar style, by Karl Lagerfeld, is available at Nordstrom.) Ratajkowski cinched the coat at the waist with a matching leather belt. She styled it with more leather in the form of slouchy square-toe knee-high boots with a curved heel.

EmRata continued the monochrome palette with her accessories: a black leather shoulder bag and classic Ray-Ban sunglasses. Her only jewelry seemed to be a gold ring; as for glam, she wore her brunette hair straight down and opted for natural makeup and a glossy lip.

Gotham//Getty Images

Ratajkowski wore another black leather trench coat last month, that time layering it over a black crewneck sweatshirt, baggy olive-green pants, and a pair of white-and-gray New Balance sneakers. To accessorize, EmRata opted for a black crossbody bag by Hermès, chunky gold earrings, and black oval sunglasses.

The model has been on a big coat-as-the-entire-outfit streak. Also last month, she was photographed wearing the most oversize suit jacket ever (with the baggiest pants ever), and days earlier debuted two fabulous trench coats, both in different burnt-orange hues.
