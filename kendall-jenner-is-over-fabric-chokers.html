Kendall Jenner Is Over Fabric Chokers

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Black fabric chokers have become a ubiquitous accessory over the past year or so, and Kendall Jenner is definitely part of the guilty party. But now, the model is ready to disown the trend that she so loved and helped make famous.

"It's so funny how trends come and go," the 21-year-old wrote on her website. "One minute, I'll be super into something and then next day I feel like I never want to see it again! I know, I was wearing fabric chokers all the time—and it's not to say I don't still love them—but I'm over them right now."

Getty / Splash

Jenner's comments shortly after we officially laid the choker to rest earlier this month (right after Matt Lauer wore a men's version on the Today show―yikes). But she won't be missing this accessory much, since she already appears to have replaced it with a new favorite: chain chokers. They're a more bejeweled iteration of the classic '90s-esque bauble, and Jenner often wears hers with other similar necklaces of various lengths for a stacked effect. You might've seen the model accessorize with the new fave in her recent street style looks.

Getty / Splash

"Instead of fabric, I'm obsessed with chain or diamond chokers right now," she explained on her site. "Anything mixed materials is fun, too! I love layering them on and piling as much cool jewelry on as I can. It's a fun statement without being too much."

And considering Jenner's influence on sartorial trends, it'll only be a matter of time before other women follow suit. Guess we won't be seeing any of this anymore.
