Bio Of Kristine Leahy

Kristine Leahy was born on 16th October 1986 in Chicago, USA. She attended Prairie Ridge High School, and she graduated from the Boston University by majoring in Journalism degree.

After finishing her graduation, she started to work at radio station WEEI in Boston. After two years of working at the radio station, she got a job in Fox Boston where she managed to host and cover all the sports news.

In the year 2012, she moved to Los Angeles and started to host programs for CBC affiliation network. Having worked for big sports centers like FOX, CBC, and NBC the reporter earns a yearly salary of $20,000, which adds value to her net worth of $2 million

Insight Into Career

Kristine is most known for her job at Fox Sports 1’s radio show The Herd with Colin Cowherd

After giving it all to her loved show, the host decided to move towards the next chapter in her life. Thus, she left the show in 2018 and started hosting the show entitled Fair Game in Fox Sports 1.

Well, the show which premiered on 22nd October 2018 had an exciting lineup with guest like Clippers coach Doc Rivers, former NFL player Ray Rice, Olympic gold medalist Nastia Liukin, and actor Josh Duhamel gracing the show.

While talking to NewYork Post, Kristine revealed that they wanted to dig deep inside the player’s story rather than the usual focus on highlights of a game.
