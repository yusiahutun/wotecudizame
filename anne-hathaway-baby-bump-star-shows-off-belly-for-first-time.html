Anne Hathaway Shows Off Baby Bump in Public for the First Time Since Announcing Shes Pregnant Aga

Publish date: 2024-05-03

Of course she’s smiling! Anne Hathaway was glowing on the red carpet, as she showed off her baby bump in public for the first time since announcing she is expecting her second child with husband, Adam Shulman.

The 36-year-old attended the opening of Broadway play Sea Wall/A Life on Thursday, August 8, in New York City, and hit up the red carpet looking fabulous. The actress was seen in a hot pink dress with a high neck — she also put her pregnant belly on display. The Oscar winner revealed another baby is on the way on July 24 via Instagram. “It’s not for a movie …⁣⁣ #2⁣” the A-lister wrote on the social media platform, addressing her bump featured in the selfie she shared. “All kidding aside, for everyone going through infertility and conception hell, please know it was not a straight line to either of my pregnancies. Sending you extra love.” So kind!

Anne and her man, 38, welcomed their first child, Jonathan Rosebanks Shulman, now 3, in 2016, four years after saying I do to each other. It wasn’t long after giving birth that the star’s perspective of being a mom changed. “I’m learning. It’s a new set of muscles,” she told People.
