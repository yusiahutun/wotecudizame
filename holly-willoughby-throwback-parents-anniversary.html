HOLLY Willoughby today shared a sweet throwback picture of her parents to mark their 43rd wedding anniversary.

The 39-year-old TV star is unable to be with her mum Linda and dad Terry due to lockdown, so instead paid tribute on Instagram.

Her folks were wed in 1977, four years before Holly was born in 1981.

To celebrate this milestone, the This Morning host posted a sweet black and white picture of them on their wedding day.

The picture shows the happy couple sharing a kiss on their special day.

Holly wrote next to it:"Happy 43rd wedding anniversary to these love birds... love you Mumma and Dadda!"

Holly is known for being extremely close to her parents.

Just last month the kindhearted star revealed how worried she was for her 72-year-old mum when coronavirus started sweeping Britain.

She voiced her concerns about her parents during a phone-in about the disease with a doctor on This Morning.

At the time Holly told Dr Claire: "My mum is older than me, she’s 72, so she’s creeping into that age bracket and my dad.
