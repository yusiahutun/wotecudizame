Is SockShare safe to use?

Users are keen to find out the kind of security available for the sites they visit. SockShare is no exception, and online users may have questions about the site. Does SockShare have viruses? Is SockShare trustworthy?

Since the content displayed on the SockShare site is not hosted on their servers, it might not be easy to tell the kind of security that these sites have to offer. Some may be full of viruses and malicious software that may target online users, so users are advised to exercise caution as they visit these third-party links.

Is SockShare legal?

SockShare maintains that it is a legal site given that all the content found on its site contains links to other sites on the internet like myspace.com, dailymotion.com, zshare.net, filefactory.com, tudou.com, uploaded.net, 2shared.com, veoh.com, youtube.com, mediafire.com, crunchyroll.com, sevenload.com, peteava.ro, stage6.com, youku.com, 4shared.com among others.
