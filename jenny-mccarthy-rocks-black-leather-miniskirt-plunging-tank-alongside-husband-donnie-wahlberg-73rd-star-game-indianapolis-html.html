Jenny McCarthy rocks black leather miniskirt and plunging tank top alongside husband Donnie Wahlberg

Publish date: 2024-07-25

The NBA paused its 2023-24 season for the 73rd NBA All-Star game on Sunday at Gainbridge Fieldhouse in Indianapolis, Indiana, home of the Indiana Pacers.

Many of the world's greatest players were on hand to showcase their skills, which from the looks of the play and the final score was all about the offense.

Among the many celebrities and high-profile people to watch all the high-flying shooting were Donnie Wahlberg and his wife Jenny McCarthy, who could be seen cheering from their courtside seats.

Considered a Boston Celtics superfan, Wahlberg, a Boston, Massachusetts native, had two of his hometown stars to make the Eastern Conference: Jayson Tatum, who was among the five starters and Jaylen Brown, who came off the bench.

The hot and heavy couple both opted for a youthful look in the fashion department with the former MTV host and Playboy Playmate dressed in a black leather miniskirt with a matching fitted top that help highlight her ample cleavage.

Jenny Mccarthy, 51, and husband Donnie Wahlberg, 54, were among the celebrities and high-profile people to attend the 73rd NBA All-Star Game in Indianapolis, Indiana on Sunday

McCarthy, 51, covered part of the black ensemble at times by wearing a purple-ish tracksuit jacket with double white stripes down the top of each sleeve.

In keeping with the color theme, she also wore black leather boots, dark sheer stockings, all while she carried a stylish black leather purse over her right shoulder. 

Rounding out her overall look, the Evergreen Park, Illinois, native had her trademark blonde tresses styled with a healthy dose of waves and volume with a part on the left.

At one point, before all the action kicked-off on the hardwood, the couple stood behind one of the backboards on the court and posed for a round or two of photos.

The New Kids On The Block star also came dressed in black pants and a matching t-shirt, white sneakers, and on occasion her slipped on a light blue 'State' jacket.

He topped his ensemble off with a black baseball cap over his closely cropped hair.

The couple, along with the thousands in attendance and millions around the world, got to witness a history-making show as the Eastern Conference defeated the Western Conference 211 to 186, in what turned out to be the highest scoring NBA All-Star Game of all time.

Wahlberg watched as Celtics small forward Jayson Tatum notched 20 points as a starter for the East, while Boston's other player to make it to the midseason showdown, Jaylen Brown scored 36 points, grabbed 8 rebounds and had 3 assists.

The former MTV host and Playboy Playmate was her usual animated self while watching all the history-making offense on the hardwood

After meeting during a joint appearance on Watch What Happens Live back in 2012, the couple started dating the following year, and were eventually married in August 2014

Damian Lillard, of the Milwaukee Bucks, was named MVP of the All-Star game after he notched 39 points, 6 assists and 3 rebounds in the Eastern Conference's win 211-186 over the West 

Along with Wahlberg and McCarthy, some of the other celebs to be in attendance included Spike Lee, Chris Tucker, Bill Murray, AJ McLean, Jennifer Hudson, 2 Chainz, Oscar Robertson, Jalen Rose, Rich Playa, Fat Joe, Babyface and Russell Wilson, among others.

The East's guard Damian Lillard, of the Milwaukee Bucks, took home the MVP honors by leading the Eastern Conference with 39 points, to go along with his 6 assists and 3 rebounds.

Lillard's MVP announcement was met with boos from the Indianapolis crowd, who wanted to see Pacers star Tyrese Haliburton take home the trophy after he had a line of 32 points, 7 rebounds and 6 assists in the win.

Sparks first flew between the television personality and the New Kids On The Block star during a joint appearance on Watch What Happens Live back in 2012.

The following year, Wahlberg appeared on McCarthy's VH1 talk show, The Jenny McCarthy Show in March 2013.

A few months later, they were officially dating and making red carpet appearances together, and in April 2014, Wahlberg popped the question.

The pair ended up getting married that August in a ceremony featured on their A&E reality show: Donnie Loves Jenny. 

The couple eventually moved to McCarthy's home state of Illinois with their blended family, consisting of McCarthy's son Evan, whom she shares with her ex-husband, John Asher, and Wahlberg's sons, Xavier Alexander and Elijah Hendrix, whom he shares with his ex-wife, Kimberly Fey, according to People.
