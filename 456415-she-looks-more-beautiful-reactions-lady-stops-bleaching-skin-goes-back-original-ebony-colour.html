Lady Stops Bleaching Skin, Wows Netizens with Original Ebony Colour: &quot;Looks More Beautiful&quot;

Publish date: 2024-02-26
  • A lady from Nigeria has been praised online after she came out to share that she stopped bleaching her skin
  • In a post shared in a closed group on Facebook, the lady shared before and after photos showing how she got back her melanated skin
  • Several users have commended her for her decision, with many saying she looked more beautiful as a dark-skinned woman

A woman has delighted netizens with before and after photos showing his skin transformation when she started bleaching.

According to the photos shared on a private Facebook, the lady stopped lightening her skin, and it went back to its original state.

The photos were reshared by Instagram blogger @africangirlskillingit, showing people the lady's transformation with her skin.

In the after photos, the unidentified lady appeared several shades darker from her bleached skin photo.
