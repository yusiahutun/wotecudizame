Jennifer Lopez's Latest Honeymoon Outfit Involves a Sheer Blouse

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Paris seems to be providing fertile ground for Jennifer Lopez's luxe street style to blossom.

In her latest honeymoon outing with husband Ben Affleck, the multi-hyphenate star stepped out in the city looking glamorous in a nearly sheer, blush-pink pussy bow blouse. The statement piece included the signature collar, as well as ruffled frills down the center and flowing bell sleeves. She paired the top with light-wash bell-bottom jeans and nude platform heels with PVC straps.

Accessories included a pair of oversized, amber-tinted circle sunglasses and an embroidered caramel leather shoulder bag with a gold chain handle.

Affleck, too, looked stylish. The actor wore a plain white T-shirt under a matching button-up, plus gray trousers and white sneakers.

Pierre Suu//Getty Images

Pierre Suu//Getty Images

Lopez has pulled out all the sartorial stops during her Parisian getaway with Affleck since the two got married in a surprise Las Vegas wedding earlier this month.

Last night, the "Love Don't Cost a Thing" singer channeled Audrey Hepburn while getting dinner near the Eiffel Tower, wearing a clingy black maxi dress and a necklace composed of large pearl beads. In another outing to the Louvre, Lopez dazzled in a vibrant halter sundress, which she paired with nude platform heels and a coordinating red handbag.

Florals have also seemed to be a pillar of style inspiration for Lopez's French holiday. She's been spotted wearing the bold, feminine print on multiple occasions throughout her and Affleck's honeymoon.
