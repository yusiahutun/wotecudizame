Greta Lee's best fashion moments

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Getty Images

Loewe

Amy Sussman//Getty Images

For this year's Critics Choice Awards, Greta Lee gave us a lesson in smart casual dressing. In custom Loewe menswear, the actress wore black shimmering tailored trousers with a matching button-up shirt. Styled with statement Tiffany & Co. earrings and strappy heels, her look is one we will be emulating in our own wardrobes.

Loewe

Monica Schipper//Getty Images

One of the many reasons we love Lee's style is her ability to have fun with fashion and not take the red carpet too seriously. For the BAFTA Tea Party, Lee wore head-to-toe Loewe in the form of a silky white dress with a statement draping bag and the house's instantly-recognisable cartoonish accessories.

Bottega Veneta

Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

Striking in red, Lee's Governors Awards Bottega Veneta look was one of our favourites. With a sculptural silhouette and a futuristic aesthetic, the brand's 'Boucle Frise' dress was the perfect partner to a pair of metallic heels.

Loewe

Monica Schipper/GA//Getty Images

Fast becoming the poster girl for Loewe, Lee had another red-carpet win in Jonathan Anderson's designs. A modern take on Old Hollywood style – thanks to her silky voluminous gown, waved cropped bob and Tiffany & Co. jewels – the actress' Golden Globes look was elegant yet statement-making.

Proenza Schouler

Michael Rowe//Getty Images

Lee knows what colours work for her, including this season's cool-girl shade red. While attending a Critics Choice Association event in celebration of cinema and television, Lee demonstrated the power in head-to-toe colour blocking in a sheer Proenza Schouler look complete with matching tights.

Loewe

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

Another powerful red moment for Lee at the 2023 Academy Museum Gala. Her off-the-shoulder Loewe silk gown with dramatic sleeves oozed elegance and sophistication, while her jewels and sleek hair kept up her cool-girl status.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Prada

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Lee regularly plays with different silhouettes and shapes, and this take on the LBD does just that. The Prada design – worn to the 2023 Women In Film Honours – is balanced by the sleek and minimal pointed pumps and thin straps.

Loewe

Variety//Getty Images

Another great statement Loewe dress. For the 2023 Gotham Awards, Lee chose to style the timeless backless design with pointed strappy heels.

Bottega Veneta

Kayla Oaddams//Getty Images

For the 2023 Variety Power Of Women event, the actress wore thigh-high metallic boots with a velvet pink mini-dress by Bottega Veneta, the ultimate party look.

The Row

Cindy Ord//Getty Images

Lee wore a statement sailor-inspired ensemble from The Row to the 2023 SCAD Savannah Film Festival. Simple yet stylish are clearly Lee's rules for getting dressed.
