Inside Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's Cozy Dinner Date at a Steak House

Publish date: 2024-06-26

After supporting Travis Kelce as he and the Kansas City Chiefs defeated the Los Angeles Chargers on Sunday, Taylor Swift enjoyed a romantic evening out with her man the following day.

The couple headed out Monday night to Piropos, an Argentine steak house near the NFL player’s Missouri home, via Kelce’s Rolls Royce. For the outing, Swift appeared to be wearing a caramel leather jacket, a black minidress, and black leather knee-high heeled boots, while Kelce wore a navy blue track suit with white stripes down the sides, according to photos published by the Daily Mail.

The Mail also reported that the two wined and dined for about two hours, with Piropos staying open past its typical 9:00 p.m. closing time to accommodate the lovebirds. At one point during the dinner date, Swift and Kelce had the entire restaurant to themselves.

Photos from the night show the duo’s sweet PDA, with the two holding hands and Kelce placing his hand on the small of Swift’s back.

Gotham//Getty Images

A source for the Daily Mail recently opened up about how Swift and Kelce’s relationship has progressed over the last month.

“Taylor loves going to his games. She really enjoys them, and she thinks it is beyond sexy that he does what he does,” the insider told the outlet earlier this week. “This is such new territory for her. She wants to support her boyfriend and she does.”

Her presence at his games has been a welcome sight. The insider added, “Since Taylor has been coming to the games, the Chiefs have won the past four consecutive games and Travis is telling Taylor that she is his lucky charm. She literally brings light into the stadium with her. His teammates have joked that she is their unofficial mascot.”
