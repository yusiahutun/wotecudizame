My Go-To Puffer Coat Keeps Me Warm Even With Just a Bra Underneath

Publish date: 2024-06-11

As we dive deeper into the holiday months, naturally, my outfit choices go one of two ways — dressy and ready for a festive outing or lowkey and comfy like an oversized hoodie or a puffer jacket when I need to leave the comfort of my home. 

I spend a sizeable amount of my day outdoors since my dog needs to go on walks every few hours, so a winter jacket that I can easily toss on to keep me warm is a must-have in my coat closet. The one I’m wearing on repeat this year is the Alo Yoga Corduroy Stage Puffer, and it’s finally back in stock. 

Alo Yoga Corduroy Stage Puffer

When I’m lounging around my apartment, I typically opt for something comfortable like a stretchy pair of leggings and, at times, just a sports bra (I run hot, so this is a commonly occurring outfit for me). When nature calls and my dog needs to head outside for a bathroom break, I toss on my Alo Yoga puffer jacket and a pair of slip-on shoes and head out the door. 

The corduroy style keeps me toasty warm, and yes, even with nothing but a bra on underneath the jacket. I’ve worn this combo on numerous occasions in freezing temperatures and blustery winds. It has been incredibly comfortable and, in complete honesty, puts many of my other down jackets to absolute shame. 

Aside from wearing the jacket while walking my dog, the sporty silhouette looks sleek and elevated, even with fancier outfits. The corduroy texture has a silky sheen that gives it a luxurious look, and the zippered pockets are my favorite feature since I can fit all of my essentials, like my phone, wallet, and keys, with additional room to spare. 

While I typically get a size large in coats, I snagged this one in an XL for a roomier fit to layer over my thicker sweaters without feeling constricted. As for the sizing, it’s a “sweet spot,” as one shopper put it, between fitted and oversized, thanks to its boxier cut. It’s available in sizes small through 2XL and four colors: white, black, green, and mauve. 

Shoppers are also big fans of the outerwear style, with one person saying it’s “like wearing a pillow all day.” Another reviewer shared a detail that I personally didn’t know — an internal zippered pocket that can hold your phone (I will be checking this out ASAP). They also applauded the jacket for being “comfortable and warm.”
