She shocked her fans with her surprise marriage to Adam Brody.

Now in equally low key fashion, Leighton Meester has revealed her wedding band - and her equally jaw-dropping engagement ring - for the very first time.

The actress delicately crossed her hands to expose the delicate ring as she posed with co-stars James Franco and Chris O'Dowd to promote their upcoming Broadway revival of John Steinbeck's Of Mice And Men in New York.

The ring alongside the diamond that marked the couple's engagement, making for a sparkling, expensive looking duo on the pretty brunette's left hand.

There it is! Leighton Meester finally flashed her wedding ring alongside her engagement ring from Adam Brody as she posed at the photo call for Broadway's Of Mice And Men in New York on Thursday

Bling! The 27-year-old former Gossip Girl star elegantly showed off her pretty pair of rings

The 27-year-old Gossip Girl star had every reason to be sporting a radiant grin as she stepped out for a photo call as a newlywed not long after she and The OC star tied the knot.

Standing between her two male co-stars, Leighton was stylish in a black sweater with a white shirt underneath and grey slacks.

For a pop of color, the brunette wore a pair of red heels.

The star kept her brown locks casually styled with natural makeup and a slick of light brown lipstick.

On February 18 Brody confirmed he and Leighton were married when he replied with a brief 'yes' after being asked if it was true if he really got married.

The happy couple tied the knot only three months after news of their engagement surfaced.

The stars - who worked together on the 2011 film The Oranges - only went public with their romance last February.

Stage left: James Franco, Leighton Meester and Chris O'Dowd joined forces at a press conference for Of Mice And Men in New York on Thursday

Adam and Leighton were originally thought to be planning to get married this summer, after her Broadway run ended.

Meanwhile, James Franco, 35, looked like like he needed a good rest on a tropical beach as he stood nearly stone face and quite bleary eyed during the press conference.

His co-star Leighton Meester, 27, and Chris O'Dowd, 34, appeared fully rested, however.

Live theater: The Broadway revival of the classic John Steinbeck play starts previews on March 19

Sporting some facial scruff and looking pale, James was casually dressed.

He wore a black cardigan, plaid shirt, loose fitting jeans and crisp white sneakers.

Chris channeled his inner hipster with a beard, navy jacket, dark denim jeans and rugged brown boots.

The trio of thespians are all making their Broadway debuts in the revival of the classic John Steinbeck play.

Tackling the stage: It is the Broadway debut of all three actors who have already conquered film and television

Oscar nominee Franco is set to play George, with O'Dowd as Lennie. The two are migrant workers in search of a better life during the Great Depression.

The American classic, which has not been seen on Broadway in 40 years, will be directed by Tony winner Anna D. Shapiro.

Of Mice And Men begins previews on March 10 with opening night set for April 16.

Leighton will have a small part as the character of Curley's Wife.

George and Lennie: The actors will portray the two migrant workers during the Depression in the classic story

Getting ready: Newly married, Leighton posted a photo of herself with the Steinbeck novel last week

It's official: It was announced by Adam Brody himself that he and Leighton Meester had secretly wed in early 2014

Limited engagement: The play runs for 18 weeks until July 27
