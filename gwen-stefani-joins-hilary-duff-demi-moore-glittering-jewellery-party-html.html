Gwen Stefani joins Hilary Duff and Demi Moore at glittering jewellery party

Publish date: 0001-01-01

An A-list crowd helped jewellery designer Nikki Erwin launch her new venture on Thursday night, with Gwen Stefani checking out the gems alongside the likes of Hilary Duff and Demi Moore.

When it comes to starry crowds, the launch couldn't have wished for a better turn-out, as the VIPs glammed up for the party hosted by Erin and Sara Foster in West Hollywood, California.

45-year-old Gwen showed off her quirky style at the launch, with her monochrome look perfectly complimenting Erwin's edgy designs.

A-list crowd: Gwen Stefani hit the launch part for Established Jewelry by Nikki Erwin on Thursday night, looking chic in a typically fashion-forward look

The platinum blonde teamed a sheer black shirt with loose-fitting trousers and a black and white furry wrap casually thrown over her shoulders.

Gwen finished off her fashion forward ensemble with some killer heeled ankle boots, gold clutch bag and of course, a slick of her favourite red lippie.

The mother of of three is clearly enjoying some time off after a busy 2014.

VIPs only: Stylist Rachel Zoe (left) and Hilary Duff (right) also lent their celeb support to the starry launch

Blonde connection: Gwen and Hilary were spotted catching up while checking out the jewellery 

Hollywood fans: Gwen, Hilary and Rachel excitedly browsed the stunning products from Nikki Erwin

The singer has been replaced after her single season stint as a judge on The Voice, with original judge Christina Aguilera taking over after a two-season hiatus.

As well as enjoying the party lifestyle, the free time is also allowing Gwen to put the finishing touches on her forthcoming album, her first solo release since The Sweet Escape in 2006.

Sia, Calvin Harris, Ryan Tedder,and Charlie XCX and among her collaborates on the as yet unnamed album, due for release later this year.

Seen something you like? Designer Nikki Erwin shows off her edgy pieces to keen shopper Hilary 

Another fan: Demi Moore also stopped by the party and seemed taken with a multi-pendant necklace

What a guestlist: Party host Sara Foster (far left) posed with Demi, Tallulah Belle Willis, Nikki Erwin and co-host and sister Erin Foster

The No Doubt frontwoman was spotted catching up with fellow blonde Hilary Duff at the party.

The 27-year-old filed from divorce from her husband of five years Mike Comrie last month, but seemed in great spirits as she had a girls night with a star-studded crowd at the jewellery launch.
