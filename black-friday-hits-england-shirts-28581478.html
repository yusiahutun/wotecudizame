Black Friday hits England shirts at Sports Direct ahead of massive USA World Cup tie

Publish date: 2024-08-02

England fans can get money back in their pocket after forking out on kit ahead of tonight's game against USA.

Black Friday has arrived at SportsDirect.com where shoppers can land a £20 voucher for spending over £100 before the end of the month.

The deal means snapping up some Three Lions World Cup gear such as the Nike home or away shirts, pre-game top or jacket as seen on TV earlier this week will save money on your next shop.

Even so, if Southgate's lads thumping Iran 6-2 on Monday was not enough to have fans flocking to the latest shirts, Black Friday might give the extra nudge needed.

The home shirt has been widely mocked for looking like an emptying toothpaste tube thanks to its fading blue shoulders but will that really stop you backing the boys?

At £74.99 for an adult jersey –the same as the devilishly good looking red counterpart – fans will need to find something at least £25 to bump up their order for a voucher.

Personalising the shirt with a name and number(s) straight away eats up a tenner, whether you go for Kane 9 or Dad 1.
