How often should I apply tattoo lotions?

Tattoo lotions can be applied as often as three times per day, but if you find that your new ink is overly dry or irritated, experts recommend saturating the area a bit more often.

What are some of the top ingredients to look for in the best tattoo lotions?

A combination of hydrating, soothing, and preferably natural ingredients, such as shea butter, coconut oil, vitamin E, aloe, and lavender are ideal for speeding up the healing process of a new tattoo.
