Kardashian fans rattled after Kris Jenner brutally snubs daughter Kourtney during speech at Christma

Publish date: 2024-03-12

KRIS Jenner has brutally snubbed her daughter Kourtney Kardashian while giving a speech at the family's Christmas party.

The Kardashians star praised the success of her daughters Kim, Khloe, Kylie and Kendall Jenner's various brands on Tuesday night.

The famous family put on a lavish bash for employees at Kylie Cosmetics, Good American and SKIMS.

Kourtney appeared not to attend the party, which was at a restaurant and stocked with Kendall's 818 tequila cocktails.

Kris stood up at one point during the dinner to honor her daughters, saying: "I've got Good American on the outside, I've got SKIMS on the inside.

"I'm drinking 818 like crazy!"

Kylie could be heard asking off-camera: "What about Kylie Cosmetics?"

Kris conceded: "I have my makeup on, Kylie Cosmetics.

"And of course, I wash my face with Kim's SKKN. I mean, does it get any better? I've got all of it."

Kris made no mention of Kourtney's long-running lifestyle website, Poosh, or Lemme, the supplement brand she launched several months ago.

The snub didn't go unnoticed by fans, with one asking on Instagram: "What about Poosh?"

Another posted, "Poor Kourtney!" while a third commented: "Nobody cares about Poosh and Lemme."

Kourtney has missed several family parties in recent months amid fan speculation that she is distancing herself from her famous family.

Rumblings of a sibling rivalry were sparked by Kourtney missing Kylie's lavish 25th birthday party on a giant yacht in August.

The festivities included a special fireworks show, the opening of personalized presents, and plenty of liquor.

Kim took part in the drinking circle and downed a shot with the birthday girl.

Kourtney lives in a $9million mansion in Calabasas with her three children - Mason, 12, Penelope, 10, and Reign, seven.

She is married to Travis Barker, who shares his son Landon, 19, and daughter Alabama, 16, with Shanna Moakler.
