50 Cent Announces The Final Lap World Tour

Publish date: 2024-06-16

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Grammy-winning rap icon Curtis “50 Cent” Jackson announced plans for a global tour to mark the 20th anniversary of his breakout album Get Rich or Die Tryin’.

Produced by Live Nation and presented by Sire Spirits, 50 Cent’s The Final Lap Tour includes a North American segment with shows in markets such as Toronto, Brooklyn, Atlanta, Houston, as well as European shows in Amsterdam, Oslo, Paris and other cities. Additional markets will be announced in the coming weeks.

The tour kicks off in North America at the Maverik Center in Salt Lake City on July 21st and wraps on September 17th at the Pine Knob Music Theatre in Detroit, Michigan.

50 Cent then heads to Europe with performances starting at the Ziggo Dome in Amsterdam on September 28th and concluding at Resorts World Arena in Birmingham, UK, on November 12th.

For the tour, 50 Cent will be relying on Ticketmaster’s Verified Fan scheme and fans can register now through Sunday, May 7th at 11:59 PM ET for a chance to purchase passes for North American shows with presales starting on May 10th.

For European fans, tickets will be available starting with local presales on Wednesday, May 10th at 10am local time and available to the general public on Friday, May 12th at 10am local time on 50Cent.com.
