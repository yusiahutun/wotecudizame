How to Create Your Own Apple Music Radio Station

Publish date: 2024-07-27

If you have an Apple Music subscription and want to discover new music, Apple Music’s “radio stations” can help.

While Apple Radio stations include actual radio station programming, some of these “stations” are simply themed collections curated by another user or Apple’s algorithm.

Did you know you can also create your own Apple Music Radio station in just a couple of easy steps? Keep reading to find out how.

Why Should You Create an Apple Music Station?

Apple Music offers a few different ways to discover music, including listening to the existing radio stations and curated playlists, besides creating your own station. Try exploring Apple Music playlists to discover new songs.

Apple Music offers the option to create a station based on a specific song, artist, or genre. The algorithm will select other songs that may be similar to the song you chose. Doing this means you can play music continuously without choosing a new playlist or album.

How to Create an Apple Music Station on Your Phone or Tablet

Creating stations is one of those Apple Music features you didn't know about, but it's actually easier than you might think. This is what you need to do:

  • To create an Apple Music station using your phone or tablet, simply play or find a song you like.
  • Then select the ellipsis (...) on the right side.
  • You will see several options; choose Create Station.
    • Close

    If you know the name of a song you want to build an Apple Radio Station around, you can simply ask Siri to “start a station from…” and then name a song, artist, or even a genre that you already have in your library. Apple Music will build a station around it, including music that isn’t in your library.

    How to Create an Apple Music Station on Your Computer

    Creating a station on your computer is just as easy. All you need to do is open Apple Music and follow these steps:

  • Use the Browse section to find some music you want to use to start a station.
  • Click on the ellipsis (...) on the right side of the song.
  • Choose Create Station from your menu of choices.

    • Start Using Apple Music Radio Stations

    If you hear any music that you like, be sure to add it to your library or “like” it so you can easily find the song, artist, or album later. You can also tell Siri whether you like or dislike the song to fine-tune your recommendations.

    If you like the station you just created, you can listen to it again. Find it by looking in the Radio menu select the recently played section. And if you are interested in making the best use of Apple Music, there are other essential tips you should try.
