Vanderpump Villa is a reality TV series following Lisa Vanderpump, a frestauranteur and former cast member on the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, and the staff of her luxury French villa, Chateau Rosabelle. The series, which the third spin-off of Vanderpump Rules following Jax & Brittany Take Kentucky and The Valley, is set in Mazerolles-du-Razès in the South of France and centers around Vanerpump’s “hand-picked staff” as they “live, work and play together, while navigating every desire of their well to-do guests,” according to the show’s description.
