RHOCheshire's Lystra says tension spills off-camera and she doesn't speak to co-stars

Publish date: 2024-07-25

The Real Housewives of Cheshire first appeared on screens in the UK on 12 January 2015 and has been entertaining audiences across the nation ever since.

The ITVBe programme which belongs to the Real Housewives' franchise gives viewers an insight into the the lavish and often scandalous lives of the highly affluent women of Cheshire.

Business owner and model Lystra Adams first joined the ladies for the 12th series of the show and hasn't looked back since.

When Lystra, 44, popped up on the Cheshire scene, the star made an explosive entrance and soon became the talk of the town.

Speaking exclusively to Daily Star, the mother-of-one ensured us that everything viewers see is very real and the tension between cast members continues even when the cameras have stopped rolling.
