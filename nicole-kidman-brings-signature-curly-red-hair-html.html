Nicole Kidman brings back her signature curly red hair

Publish date: 2024-06-13

Nicole Kidman has gone back to the iconic look of her early years as an actress.

The Australian-born Hollywood star, 55, has replaced the long golden locks she flaunted at the 95th Academy Awards with curly red hair. 

Posting images of the drastic make over to Instagram on Wednesday, the Nine Perfect Strangers star flaunted her fresh strawberry blonde tresses. 

She captioned the reel of photos with three sparkling star emojis as she matched her new hair colour with a lacy, light blue dress. 

Kidman looked incredible at the Oscars in Los Angeles just over a week ago as she stepped out in a black slinky sequinned gown by Armani Privé.
