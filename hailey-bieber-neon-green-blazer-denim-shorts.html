Hailey Bieber Pairs a Neon-Green Blazer with Denim Short Shorts

Publish date: 2024-06-08

Hailey Bieber always looks ready for summer.

Yesterday, the model was photographed leaving a business meeting in Beverly Hills sporting an oversize lime-green blazer by luxury womenswear label Kwaidan Editions with cross-front denim shorts from EB Denim and a smocked white camisole from Totême.

Having mastered the art of color coordinating, Bieber and her longtime stylist, Maeve Reilly, paired the model's neon outerwear with accessories in similar shades. Bieber elevated the relatively casual outfit with a pair of Bottega Veneta's strappy leather Stretch Sandals and a mini grass-green woven clutch from the powerhouse brand to match. As for the rest of the look, the model stayed true to her signature style with loose beach waves and layered gold jewelry for a finishing touch.

BACKGRID//BACKGRID

By now, Bieber is somewhat of a street style fixture, having produced one runway-approved outfit after the other. Whether she's out and about with famous friends like Kendall Jenner and Justine Skye on the West Coast, or enjoying a date night in New York City with her husband, Justin Bieber, the model always rocks a stunning ensemble to mark the occasion.

In the last few weeks alone, Mrs. Bieber has been seen wearing everything from double denim and a plunging bustier for a night out with her hubby to a nude midi dress that showcased her penchant for monochromatic dressing. With each cool-girl outfit, the model further proves that she has the range to pull off just about any look.
