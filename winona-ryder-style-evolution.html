Winona Ryder's Style Evolution Through the Years

Publish date: 2024-05-29
1

1987

Ron Galella, Ltd.//Getty Images

Ryder kept it cozy and casual for the Amazing Chuck and Grace premiere. The look she went for included a printed maxi dress and an oversized gray wool overcoat. In addition, her fringe bangs added the perfect amount of edge.

2

1988

Ron Galella//Getty Images

The actress smiled alongside actor Rob Lowe at the Golden Globe Awards in a red velvet dress matched with a boxy black blazer. Her red lipstick and small beaded necklace were nice touches to the elegant ensemble.

3

1989

Ron Galella, Ltd.//Getty Images

Ryder has always been the queen of street style. She’s pictured here with the Beastie Boys’ Ad-Rock wearing a slouchy light pink sweater, dark wash jeans, and a simple black flap handbag.

4

1989

Ron Galella//Getty Images

Ryder proved less is more when she arrived at the Independent Film Spirit Awards in a simple white dress with a flouncy pleated skirt. And, yes, that’s Keanu Reeves beside her.

5

1989

Vinnie Zuffante//Getty Images

The actress donned a sleek and structural blazer dress paired with sheer tights for the premiere of Batman.

6

1989

Kevin.Mazur//Getty Images

For a 1989 music video titled “Debbie Gibson Is Pregnant with My Two-Headed Love Child,” Ryder was put in a silky bridal gown complete with veil.

7

1990

Ron Galella//Getty Images

Ryder posed with her Edward Scissorhands co-star in a black leather trench coat layered over sheer black stockings. She styled the all-black sartorial statement with a dark red lip and a chic black bob.

8

1992

Ron Galella, Ltd.//Getty Images

You can always count on Ryder to wear something innovative or unexpected to the red carpet. She was at the Dracula premiere wearing a ruffled two-piece skirt set in a soothing dark blue hue. The outfit had a specific Victorian-era feel to it.

9

1993

Ron Galella, Ltd.//Getty Images

She attended the Age of Innocence red carpet in another vampy look. This one included a deep purple velvet dress with a simple choker and minimal makeup.

10

1994

Ron Galella//Getty Images

How amazing is this fringed gown she wore to the 1994 Oscars?

11

1994

Ron Galella, Ltd.//Getty Images

She sported more velvet at the premiere of Little Women. This time, it consisted of a hip-length velveteen jacket layered over a voluminous black skirt.

12

1996

Jim Smeal//Getty Images

Ryder looked like she stepped right out of an old Hollywood movie when she showed up to the Fire and Ice Ball in a timeless black gown with an exquisitely designed neckline and fitted waist.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

1996

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

She rocked a similar look at an event in Los Angeles. Ryder paired a gown with oversized pearl and diamond earrings and a slicked-back updo.

14

1996

Jim Smeal//Getty Images

For the Academy Awards, she went with a flapper-esque ensemble that consisted of a sequined chiffon beige dress with a matching stole. Her wavy hair and satin pouch handbag made things look extra romantic.

15

1997

Ron Galella, Ltd.//Getty Images

Here, Ryder wore a Barbie pink satin gown with a chiffon shawl, statement-making diamond earrings, and a rose pink envelope clutch.

16

1997

Kevin.Mazur//Getty Images

The actress can never say no to a head-turning lace look. This dress featured a ruffled see-through collar and a sparkly skirt.

17

1998

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

The Cannes Film Festival always has fantastic looks, like this sequined getup Ryder wore with shiny silver sandals and a bedazzled hair accessory.

18

1998

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Here’s Ryder in another excellent Cannes Film Festival ensemble comprised of a purple gown matched with a chiffon stole, a layer of necklaces, and a beaded handbag.

19

1998

Ron Galella//Getty Images

For the premiere of Smoke Signals, the actress stepped out in a pretty periwinkle dress with a high neckline and slightly ruffled hem.

20

1998

Jeff Kravitz//Getty Images

She wore an all-black look for the 1998 MTV Music Video Awards. It featured a sleeveless top, satin pants, and square-framed sunglasses. Her platform flip-flops and sweater tied around her waist gave her an effortlessly cool vibe.
