Only Fans star Lana Wolf joins charity football match

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Rangers-mad OnlyFans star, and Daily Star favourite, Lana Wolf will take to the football field in August.

Wolf, who is also big on TikTok, will be taking part in the Scottish TikTokers charity football match to raise money for Glasgow Children's Hospital charity.

And it takes place at New Douglas Park, the home of Scottish Championship side Hamilton Academical.

Fans can watch Wolf take to the field for just £10, in a match organised by fellow TikTok star AJ Greenway.

But it's Wolf's shirt number which has been a talking point for some on social media.

One Twitter user, fellow Rangers fan Captain_Morgan16 wrote: “Rumour has it you’ve been given number 69.”

Her participation in charity game for a children's hospital did also raise a few eyebrows, too.

Gavin Currie tweeted: “Something offside about pornstars playing in kids charity matches.”

And another wrote: “How the f**k is she playing in a kids hospital charity match?”

She hasn't yet posted about the match to her 116.6k TikTok fans, but others joining her on the pitch include Stephanie Wightman, Jane Park and Angryplumber84.

This news comes just only a few days after Lana revealed she was forced to miss Rangers' Europa League round of 16 triumph against Red Star Belgrade in Serbia as she was moving house.

She told her thousands of followers that she was unable to travel to Serbia in time for Rangers match against Red Star Belgrade tonight (Thursday, March 17).

But the model has a pretty good excuse for missing her home team's match and said she has achievements of her own that she must see to first.

She tweeted: “Really wish I was in Serbia but I get my new house keys tomorrow."

In response, Lana was flooded with congratulations messages, which prompted her to make a last ditch request.

She then issued a plea to her 65,000 followers and asked whether anyone would be able to help her with the daunting task.

"Anyone got a big van that can help me move some stuff tomorrow?" she asked in a follow-up tweet.

The model confessed that she hasn't been organised with the move and said she had left it "so last minute" to call a removal firm.

The Daily Star recently reported how the model had some unusual items on her Amazon wish list which would be perfect for her new home.

Being a massive fan of Rangers FC, it is no surprise that she has asked her fans to purchase her a double duvet set with her beloved team's logo and colours on it.

The list features everything from scented candles to a Nintendo Switch games console, to help her stay entertained after her move.

She also requested a clothes steamer and several tracksuits, high-heeled boots and two bikinis that are branded with the Union Jack.
