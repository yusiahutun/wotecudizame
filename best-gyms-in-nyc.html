Publish date: 2024-03-27

Class Format: Boxing is typically not seen as female friendly. With the majority of WTF's clientele being female, there is an underlying sense of reassurance that this is something that you can excel at—and be badass at. Unlike most places, WTF does not believe in sticking you with a punching bag for an hour and call that boxing. They combine real boxing with strength training to maximize results. Classes are all 55 minutes long and focus on proper technique and form, providing trainer-guided shadowboxing, bag work, mitt work and high intensity circuit training all in one class. With our average class size being 15-18 people, the trainers teach, interact and correct every person in the room. "Our goal is for you to leave the classroom knowing more and feeling more confident than when you walked in," says Nikki Campbell, an instructor and the Director of Staff Development at WTF.

When can you start seeing results? "Mental results will happen immediately and cardiovascular results will happen in two weeks, where you will see a big jump in endurance and conditioning. Actual physical results will happen in three-four weeks. You will see your self losing weight, arms and core getting stronger and an overall health improvement. It's a mental and physical confidence that we provide that makes WTF people's second home," says Campbell.

Location: WTF; 636 Broadway #204, New York, NY 10012. For class times, click here.

Cost: Membership offers daily bootcamp classes, two small group classes (four people max.) and one partner training sessions (two people max.) every month for $197. Gloves, towels and use of the 11,000 sq-foot facility is included at no extra charge. WTF's current promo offers a first month for $97 and comes with a free pair of WTF hand wraps.
