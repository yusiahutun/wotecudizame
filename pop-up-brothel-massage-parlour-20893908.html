'Pop up brothel' in massage parlour raided after punter gives wife gonorrhoea

Publish date: 2024-03-06

A pop up brothel in a British town centre has been raided by police after one its punters gave his wife a nasty case of gonorrhoea.

Police officers who swooped on the Oriental Healthy Massage parlour in Commercial Road surprised two naked men, who were with two women to appear to have been working at the brothel in Swindon, Wilts.

A large quantity of cash was also recovered during the raid on November 13.

The raid was prompted after a local man had contracted the venereal disease in October and passed it on to his wife.

She discussed the matter with her husband, and passed on her findings to police.

Swindon magistrates have made the three-month closure order banning anyone from entering the Commercial Road premises.
