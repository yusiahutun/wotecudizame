How To Get Rid of Neck Wrinkles

Publish date: 2024-04-15

Tech Neck is a sing-songy term for a very real problem: wrinkled skin caused by constantly looking down at your phone. Once a wrinkle forms, it can be hard to erase it—especially in an area as elastic and mobile as your neck where Botox can be ineffective. But there is nothing a little filler can't fix.

New York plastic surgeon Dr. Norman Rowe gave three women the chance to correct their horizontal neck lines by using a very small dose of hyaluronic acid filler. Injected along the line, the filler plumps and fills—and the smooth results last for about a year. The procedure takes no more than ten minutes, and you can expect a little bruising and swelling to follow. After a few days, your neck wrinkles are softened or totally erased.
