When it comes to music, there is no such thing as a language barrier. Keeping that in mind, the Colombian singer, J Balvin, has leveled up his music game and garnered global attention. 

The artist creates his music in his own style that expresses his feelings and has attracted enough attention. With all of this love and respect, the public is extremely interested in his personal life, particularly his dating status and potential girlfriend.

Is J Balvin Dating? Gay Or Has A Girlfriend?

In addition to his great musical skills and performances, his admirers worldwide have wondered for a long time if he is dating or has a girlfriend.

However, the public has also assumed the singer to be gay since he often experimented with hair colors. The speculation about his sexuality arose from all of his pro-LGBTQ+ stance and gender-defying style.

Although he keeps a low profile in public, the Colombian artist is not hesitant to speak publicly about his personal life. However, J Balvin, who feels marriage is a social construct, denied being gay when he was asked to address the rumors.

Rather, he stated that he prefers a sociable and self-sufficient partner. Fortunately, Valentina Ferrer, the former Miss Argentina, met all of his expectations.

J Balvin and his girlfriend Valentina Ferrer on May 24th, 2021 (Credits:- Instagram)

In February 2018, the reggaeton star and his model girlfriend were first sighted at New York Men's Fashion Week. However, according to E! News, they initially met on Balvin’s music video shoot for Sigo Extraándote in 2017.

In the music video, his supposed model girlfriend played the role of his wife and his child's mother. But, as a surprise! it did come true in real life as well.

In April 2021, the couple revealed they were expecting their first child together in a Vogue Mexico cover. Ferrer captioned a photo with the phrase "the family is growing" on Instagram.

Further, on July 01, 2021, Ferrer shared a wholesome picture of her child on her Instagram, and the caption read- 

"4 days of the best Love..."

The newborn child of J Balvin and his girlfriend Valentina Ferrer on June 2021 (Credits:- Instagram)

With the new delight that the singer has received, he has his own share of happiness with his musical career, becoming stronger by the day.

J Balvin’s Career And Net Worth In 2021

Not only did J Balvin's love life thrive, but so did his career. His musical career has likewise soared to new heights.

He achieved platinum and multi-platinum status in the United States and Mexico by collaborating with American rapper Cardi B, Beyonce, and Flamengo vocalist Rosalia.

Despite all of the insights about his romance with his model girlfriend and his successful musical career, the public is still unaware of a few things related to him. 

Short Bio

José Álvaro Osorio Balvín, better known as J Balvin, now age 34 - was born on 7th May 1985 in Medellin, Colombia. 

His early family history shows that he had a happy childhood until his father's business failed. Aside from that, his mother was diagnosed with a rare hereditary metabolic illness called Acute Intermittent Porphyria.

J Balvin, who is five feet and seven inches (1.70 m) tall, arrived in the United States as an English exchange student. He even started to study music while serving as a dog walker in New York.

After returning home, the You Stay singer became famous with his 2010 album Real. His worldwide star status was fueled by his 2012 work La Familia.
