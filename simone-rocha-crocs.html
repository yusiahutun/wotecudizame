Simone Rocha's Crocs collection is here

Publish date: 2024-02-02

Last September, Simone Rocha unveiled her spring/summer 2024 collection, 'Dress Rehearsal' through which she introduced the world to the most beautiful take on the Croc we have ever seen. The designer gave the famously comfortable shoe a pearl-encrusted makeover that will no doubt be an absolute sell-out when it launches next week.

Described as a “creative juxtaposition of femininity and utilitarianism,” the collection consists of three styles decorated in pearls, crystal and jet beading, some with inbuilt embellished socks.

Specifically, Rocha has reimagined the Crocs Siren Clog, the Classic Clog and the Quick Trail shoes with her trademark whimsical and romantic accents, merging the distinct design style of Rocha with the iconic comfort and DNA of Crocs.

courtesy of simone rocha

The collection is just the latest creative collaboration that Rocha has taken on in recent years, having teamed up with H&M, Moncler and Jean Paul Gaultier. Giving that Simone spin to a new product is clearly something she thrives at.

“It’s a really good chance to stretch your mind in other directions,” she previously told us. “When I take on a collaboration, I always want it to be something where I can take my expertise to something new. Maybe that means doing something super high level with Moncler or perhaps super playful with Crocs, creating something en masse with H&M – which was like night and day compared with running an independent label – or really understanding a new way of working with Jean Paul Gaultier. Collaboration is just all about learning.”

The collection was also seen on Rocha's autumn/winter 2024 collection, suggesting that we may get another drop of the collaboration later in the year. But, for the time being, mark 10 April in your diary if you want to get your hands on a pair as soon as they launch.

The Simone Rocha x Crocs collection will be available to shop at Crocs, Simone Rocha and at other selected retailers.
