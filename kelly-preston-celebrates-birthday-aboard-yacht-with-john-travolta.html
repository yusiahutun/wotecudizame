John Travolta Sweetly Calls Wife Kelly Preston a Beautiful and Talented Woman on Her 57th Birt

Publish date: 2024-05-22

Best celebrity couple ever! John Travolta took to Instagram on Sunday, October 13, to wish wife Kelly Preston a happy 57th birthday — and he did it in the best way imaginable.

The dad of three shared one photo of Kelly hanging out outside and he wrote in the caption, “Happy birthday to this beautiful and talented woman! ❤️@therealkellypreston.” Then, as if that wasn’t sweet enough, he shared a different snap of him, Kelly, their 19-year-old daughter, Ella, and a couple of their friends — including Look Who’s Talking costar Kirstie Alley — aboard a yacht and wrote, “Celebrating Kelly’s birthday with family and friends 🎂.”

Kelly responded back to John’s beautiful post and commented, “Thank you honey I love you so much! 💕🌸.” Ella also shared three heart-eyed emojis in the comments section. The couple’s pal, Kevin Connolly, wrote, “Most beautiful woman in the game for all these years!!!! ❤️.”

Ella didn’t forget to wish her mom a happy birthday on social media either. The young adult shared two cute photos of her and Kelly together on Instagram. “Happy Happy Birthday Mama!! You are so beautiful, kind, talented, smart, funny and so much more!! I love you to the moon and back. 😘💛✨🌸,” she wrote.

The Twins star also responded back to her daughter’s post. “Thank you baby … I love you so much,” she replied. “You are the most magical daughter 🌸💕.”

Kelly and her family also like to celebrate all of their holidays and special occasions on social media. On Thursday, September 5, the Jerry Maguire actress took to Instagram to pay tribute to her and John’s 28th wedding anniversary.

“To my dearest Johnny, the most wonderful man I know. You have given me hope when I have felt lost, loved me patiently and unconditionally … made me laugh harder than any other human being possible … shared the most beautiful highs and at times lows,” she said.

“You’re a dream Daddio and make life so much fun!” John’s soulmate added. “I trust my love with you implicitly … with you I know I will always be okay no matter what happens … I love you forever and completely. Happy 28th Anniversary!” Aww!

We couldn’t love this family more if we tried!
