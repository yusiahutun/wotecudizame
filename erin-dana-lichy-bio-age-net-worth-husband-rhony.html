Erin Dana Lichy Husband

Erin Dana Lichy married Abraham “Abe” Lichy, son of Regla Lichy and Dr. Jacob Lichy, also of New York, during a ceremony officiated by rabbi Doron Aizenman Cipriani at Wall Street, an event space in New York. This happened likely around late June 2012.

Erin was 24 at the time and her groom was 29. Having graduated from the State University at Albany, she was still studying for a master’s degree in real estate development at New York University. He went on to gush that Erin is amazing as a wife, a mother, and a life partner. Also, he claimed she is the one who has made his and the kids’ lives beautiful to live in.

Erin and her husband share three kids. Daughter Layla turned 5 on 22 March 2022 and son Levi turned 7 years old on 8 December 2021.
