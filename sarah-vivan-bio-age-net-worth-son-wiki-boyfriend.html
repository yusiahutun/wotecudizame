Famously known as the ex-girlfriend of Lil’Wayne, Sarah Vivan is a radio personality by profession. She is currently working as an online-personality in Sirius XM from 2015. Moreover, Sarah is not only a professional radio personality but is also a loving and caring mother of her two children.

Vivan is a mother of Lil Wayne’s son named Dwayne Carter III. And also a half mother of Wayne’s another son named, Neal Carter. So far, Sarah makes a good sum throughout her career. Let’s find out more about Sarah’s love life, affairs, kids, earning, net worth and many more.

Early Life of Sarah Vivan

Born on 17th August 1987, in Cincinnati, Ohio under the birth sign Leo, Sarah Vivan is a radio personality. She is American as per nationality and as far as her ethnicity is concerned, Vivan is of Mixed ( biracial Vietnamese and white). She was born to her father, Jammie Ballew and mother Christina Tran and grew up with her sisters Elisabeth and Jayde Mauldin.

Regarding her academics, Sarah studied fashion at the ‘University of Cincinnati‘. Moreover, in 2011, she earned the degree of Radio and Television Broadcasting Technology from Atlanta Broadcasting Institute.

In an interview, Sarah claimed she never aimed to be a radio personality. In fact, she wanted to start her career in styling. But, after one of her friends insisted on her, she tried to build her career in radio and the rest we all know.

Television Career

Vivan started her career as an intern in Clear Channel Outdoor Global for about 8 months. Likewise, in September 2012, she joined iHeartMedia as a programming assistant. She rose to fame after she joined Streetz 94.5FM in 2012 as an on-air personality. While working there, she took an interview of several famous personalities.

As of 2019, she works in getting Engaged Media as an account manager and as an on-air personality at Sirius XM. In Sirius, she is hosting a program called, ‘Streetz Is Watching’. Besides, she is also a goalkeeper trainer at Inter Atlanta FC. Furthermore, she has interviewed an American comedian, actor and television host, Kevin Hart and a Canadian rapper Drake.
