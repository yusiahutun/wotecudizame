Publish date: 2024-08-06

And it makes sense: COVID-19 recovery, nutritional changes, and extreme stress are all common causes for hair loss, and there’s been no lack of all three in the past few years.

The good news is that each of these hair loss types are treatable. While it’s always best to consult with your primary care physician or dermatologist about new hair loss or thinning concerns, there are also a number of solutions available to try at home.
