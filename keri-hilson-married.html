Keri Hilson Current Relationship 

After her separation from Serge, Keri has decided to take some time off from all the relationship drama and get to know more about herself. She wants to explore various aspects of her life. 

Keri with her boyfriend Serge Ibaka during an event in Oklahoma, Source: Heavy
