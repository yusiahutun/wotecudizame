#2,621 Mardi Gras in New Orleans. Laurel Mercantile

Publish date: 2024-02-20

I’ve never, ever, been this tired. We woke up at 3:30 am central time to catch an early plane from NYC to New Orleans because we had the great pleasure of being the grand marshals for the Krewe of Morpheus parade tonight. I’ve never been to Mardi Gras in my life, and I honestly didn’t understand what it was going to be like… And then here we were, riding in it. What a day-slash-night.


We even got to see Ross and Laura along the way whom we may or may not have shown favoritism to in our throwing. And now if you will excuse me, I am going to go comatose for a solid 9 hours!
