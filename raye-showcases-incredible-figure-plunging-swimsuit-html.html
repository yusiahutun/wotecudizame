Raye has jetted off to the Maldives to enjoy a luxurious holiday following the success of her debut album. 

The singer, 25, looked incredible as she took to Instagram to give a glimpse of her stay at The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives. 

Raye sent temperatures soaring in a slew of swimwear shots which saw the songstress hit the beach in a plunging one-piece. 

The Escapism hitmaker showcased plenty of cleavage in the low-cut swimsuit which plunged to the navel. 

Raye showcased her bronzed tan and natural beauty as she wore a minimal make-up look and accessorised her beachwear with a collection of necklaces and large hoop earrings.

It provides a welcome break for Raye, who in February scored her first number one hit with the single Escapism - the lead track from her debut album, My 21st Century Blues.

The candid track sees Raye sing about trying to numb herself with alcohol and cocaine after a breakup, with the BRIT Award nominee telling MailOnline that opening up about her experience served as 'medicine'. 

Revealing that she 'feels like a new artist', Raye explained: 'This album covers some pretty intense themes and things that are unattractive to disclose as a woman. We're encouraged to keep quiet, or it's seen as embarrassing.

'For me is just very empowering, and I hope it can be medicine for anyone else who who needs it.' 

While the process of writing the songs was cathartic, it also led to candid and frank conversations with her loved ones - some of whom had no idea she had battled past demons.
