Lil Durk Finesses With OTF Gang In Hellcats &amp; Trackhawks Video

Publish date: 2024-02-25

Lil Durk’s Only The Family team isn’t resting on any success earned in 2021.

Not long after the March 5 release of the OTF compilation album Loyal Bros, Durk and crew return with a video for “Hellcats & Trackhawks,” featuring shots of Mercedes Benz Stadium in Atlanta, a fleet of Hellcat Dodge Challengers and mini-tributes to the late King Von.

“I be hatin’ when thеse rappers call these rappers brothers,” Durk defiantly raps on the single. “Only The Family in my eyes, bitch, we got each other.”
