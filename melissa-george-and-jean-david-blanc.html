About

Melissa George is a 47 year old Australian Actress. Born Melissa Suzanne George on 6th August, 1976 in Perth, Western Australia, Australia, she is famous for her roles as Angel Brooks-Parrish in the long-running Seven Network soap opera Home and Away, May in the 1998 neo-noir science fiction mystery thriller film Dark City, Jennifer "Jenny" Wilson in the 1999 mystery crime drama film The Limey, Cleo Miller in the 2001 teen black comedy film Sugar & Spice, Molly in the NBC sitcom Friends, Lauren Reed in the ABC action thriller and science fiction series Alias, Kathy Lutz in the 2005 supernatural horror film The Amityville Horror, Deanna Schine in the 2005 crime thriller film Derailed, Christine in the 2007 biographical period drama film Music Within, Laura Hill in the HBO drama series In Treatment, Sadie Harris in the ABC medical drama series Grey’s Anatomy, Clara Musso in the FOX crime drama series Lie to Me, Rosie in the ABC1 family drama miniseries The Slap as well as the NBC family drama miniseries of the same name, Samantha "Sam" Hunter/Alex Kent in the BBC spy drama series Hunted, Marilyn Garbanza in the CBS legal drama series The Good Wife, Dr. Alexandra Panttiere in the NBC medical drama series Heartbeat, Diane Hagerty in the Hulu original science fiction drama web series The First, Charlotte in the Nine Network drama series Bad Mothers, and Margot in the Apple TV+ original drama web series The Mosquito Coast in a career that spans 1993–present. Her zodiac sign is Leo.
