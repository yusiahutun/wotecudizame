Justin Timberlake's 'diabolical breakup text' to Britney Spears revealed

Publish date: 2024-04-27

It was recently revealed that Justin Timberlake broke up with Britney Spears through a text message, and now that alledged two-word breakup text message has been revealed, ahead of the release of her memoir, The Woman in Me.

The music stars from 1999 to 2002, and Britney has revealed more information on the ups and downs of their relationships, including her getting pregnant and having an abortion. Now, the text that ended it all has been revealed, and fans are in shock.

Justin Timberlake’s two-word breakup text to Britney Spears revealed

Back in the day, many fans looked up to and admired the celebrity couple‘s relationship, however, it looks like a lot more was going on behind closed doors than people thought.

The pair broke up in 2002, with Timberlake dropping a two-word text to his girlfriend, which reportedly read: “It’s over!!!” Page Six reveals.

Chris Applebaum, director of Britney’s “Overprotected: The Darkchild Remix” music video, was there at the time the star received the text, on set of the shoot.

He told the publication that Spears reportedly ‘went missing’ for 20-40 minutes during the second day of filming.

Chris told the publication he then found her in her trailer ” sitting on the floor with her legs crossed, her makeup smudged, and in tears.”

She then showed him the two-word text, and understandably, was hesitant to finish filming the rest of the video.

The understanding director told Page Six his response was: “If you don’t have it in you, I totally understand … but if you want to go out there and finish this last set-up in the rain, you can show him that he just made the biggest f****** mistake of his life.”

“You know that? That’s a good idea. I’m going to show him that he blew the best thing he ever had,” Spears replied.

Upon hearing the news of the breakup, fans took to X, formerly known as Twitter to share their views.

“So much more diabolical when you consider the year. You know it reads like ‘m brkn up w u. sry'” wrote one.

“Texting a breakup message using T9 is crazy,” wrote another.

The now 41-year-old went through a tough time during the breakup, with many media outlets portraying her as being “unfaithful,” including that famous Diane Sawyer interview.

Britney Spears says her book’s purpose ‘wasn’t to offend anyone’

Cut to the modern day Britney Spears gains a lot of attention from her ‘strange’ Instagram videos, dancing around in front of the camera.

Although the star was recently deactivated ahead of the memoir release, she’s now back in business, and recently posted a statement to her 42.2 million followers addressing the book.

In part of the statement, she penned: “My book’s purpose was not to offend anyone by any means!! That was me then…. That is in the past!! I don’t like the headlines I am reading, that’s why I quit the business 4 years ago.”

The star then explained her book was from ’20 years ago’ and she has now moved on to a “beautiful clean slate.”
