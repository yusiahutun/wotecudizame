Returned's Agnes Bruckner couldn't be more excited to have a baby with Alefaio Brewer

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Agnes Bruckner is pregnant with her first child.

According to a Friday report from People, the 30-year-old actress, who stars on A&E's hit series The Returned, is having a baby with boyfriend Alefaio Brewer. 

'From a young age, I dreamed about being a mom, and now it’s finally happening for real and I couldn’t be more excited!' the former The Bold & The Beautiful actress told the site.
