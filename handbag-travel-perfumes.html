Best travel size perfumes | Travel fragrance oils and powders

Publish date: 2024-03-03

1

Giorgio Armani

Courtesy

SHOP NOW

There's a new way to subtly scent your skin: with a perfumed powder compact. This pinky-tinted powder infused with Giorgio Armani's iconic Sì Chypre is perfect for locking in the matching perfume, or for on-the-go application. You'll feel impossibly chic doing so.

Giorgio Armani Sì Perfumed Powder, £35 at lookfantastic.co.uk

2

Anine Bing

Courtesy

SHOP NOW

This utterly addictive fragrance blends rose with musk and black pepper, presented in a mini bottle for the busy woman (like the style entrepreneur Anine Bing herself). Being a pure concentrated oil, there's as much perfume as you'd find in a big bottle, without it being spread out with alcohol. Dab and go.

Anine Bing Savage Rose Perfume Oil, £60, aninebing.com

3

Glossier

Courtesy

SHOP NOW

When it launched late in 2017 the world fell hard for Glossier You, the skin-like scent that literally smells like you (but better). The cult beauty brand has since launched a solid version - a portable and personal format designed to be kept close.

Glossier You Perfume Solid, £19, glossier.com

4

Byredo

SHOP NOW

The only thing better than a Byredo bottle on your dressing table is a Byredo kabuki in your bag. A few strokes of this soft brush deposits a transparent powder which scents the skin in either Bal d'Afrique, Blanche or Gypsy Water.

Byredo Kabuki Perfume, 7g, £48 each, libertylondon.com

5

Kilian

SHOP NOW

A Kilian fragrance is always an accessory in its own right, but let's face it, a weighty clutch case in addition to your handbag isn't exactly travel-friendly. Well, meet the travel spray version of summery scent Moonlight In Heaven, which comes in an equally chic atomiser with mother of pearl detail as well as four vials of refill scent. The juice itself blends citrus notes with coconut, mango rice and tonka bean. Dreamy.

Kilian Moonlight In Heaven Travel Spray, four 7.5ml vials, £120, harveynichols.com

6

Atelier Cologne

SHOP NOW

If you love citruses for summer, look no further than Atelier Cologne and their line-up of fresh, sparkling scents. The travel versions, in portable 30ml sprays, are available with colourful leather pouches that can be engraved with your initials at the brand's Covent Garden boutique. Forget monogrammed luggage tags, we're coveting these.

From £55 for 30ml. Coloured leather pouches, £20, are optional and come with complementary engraving, ateliercologne.com

7

Jo Loves

SHOP NOW

Meet beauty legend Jo Malone's latest fragrance innovation; the perfume paintbrush. The gel cologne formulas can be swept onto the skin to deliver scent wherever you want it. It dries in seconds but lasts for hours. Available in Pomelo, Green Orange & Coriander, Red Truffle 21 and White Rose & Lemon Leaves.

Jo Loves The Fragrance Paintbrush, 18ml, £40 each, available at cultbeauty.co.uk.

8

Trish McEvoy

SHOP NOW

This oud version of Trish McEvoy's No. 9 scent comes in precious perfume oil form, perfectly packaged for your handbag. But don't let its mini stature deceive you, a dab of the richly concentrated elixir goes a long way.

Trish McEvoy 9 Oud Oil, £175, libertylondon.com

9

Vilhelm Parfumerie

SHOP NOW

Niche New York fragrance house Vilhelm Parfumerie has a travel-friendly edit of its six best sellers, each housed in a chic and sleek hand-cut glass bottle. Available in Dear Polly, Purple Fig, Morning Chess, Dirty Velvet, A Lilac A Day and Room Service.

The Vilhelm Parfumerie Explorer Collection, each 18ml, £60, libertylondon.com
