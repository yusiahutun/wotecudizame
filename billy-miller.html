American Actor Billy Miller was born William John Miller II on 17th September, 1979 in Tulsa, Oklahoma, USA and passed away on 15th Sep 2023 Austin, Texas, USA aged 43. He is most remembered for his soap opera roles as Richie Novak in the ABC daytime soap opera All My Children, Billy Abbott in the long-running CBS daytime soap opera The Young and the Restless, and Jason Morgan and Drew Cain in the long-running ABC daytime soap opera General Hospital.
