Clint Eastwoods Son Scott Gives Advice on Accomplishing Your Dreams: Dont Take No for an An

Publish date: 2024-03-28

So helpful! Clint Eastwood‘s son Scott Eastwood shared the best advice for achieving your goals when he posted a shirtless photo of himself at the gym to Instagram on Monday, March 9.

“It’s gonna take a lot of work to accomplish what you want in this life. But it’s possible,” he wrote. “You just get up every day and keep pushing. Don’t take No for an answer. It’s like working out and being healthy, it’s about consistency. Not something you do on January 1 for a while and then stop. You make it a part of your daily routine.”

The Suicide Squad actor told his 3.4 million followers to never doubt themselves because that’s the first step to giving up. “Even when your mind has that little voice that says, ‘No stay on the couch.’ You say, ‘Beat it little voice. I’m coming for you.’ And then get your ass out in the world and get it done!!!”

He said the best way to do something that’s tough is to “make it fun” and “different.” That way it won’t feel like work. “Challenge yourself,” Scott wrote. “Most important, keep grinding and following your dreams. If you want them bad enough you will get them!!!”

We don’t know about you, but we’re feeling empowered already! The Teen Choice Award winner definitely has his father’s hard-working attitude. By Clint not giving up, he’s won two Academy Awards and four Golden Globes. In October 2019, Scott exclusively revealed to Closer Weekly the best advice he’s ever gotten from his dad.

“I will say that he tells me to listen. Listen more. Keep my mouth shut,” The Fate of the Furious star laughed. “I’m pretty good at this.”

Aside from Scott, Clint is also a proud dad to his other kids — Laurie, 66, Kimber, 55, Kyle, 51, Alison, 47, Kathryn, 32, Francesca, 26, and Morgan, 23 — from previous relationships.
