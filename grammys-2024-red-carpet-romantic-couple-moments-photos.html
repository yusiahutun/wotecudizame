17 adorable couple moments at the 2024 Grammys: Heidi Klum &amp; Tom Kaulitz, Chrissy Teigen &amp; John Lege

Publish date: 2024-08-13

The Grammy Awards red carpet was bubbling with A-list romance

    Music's biggest night finally arrived on Sunday as a who's who of artists gathered at the Crypto.com Arena in Downtown Los Angeles for the 2024 Grammy Awards.

    Of course, there was some jaw-dropping fashion on the red carpet – but there were also romantic displays of affection and cute moments between many of the A-list musicians and their supportive partners.

    From Kelly Osbourne and Slipknot's Sid Wilson making their red carpet debut to Chrissy Teigen's very affectionate display towards her husband, John Legend, and David Guetta placing a protective hand on his pregnant girlfriend Jessica Ledon's bump, take a look at some of the most adorable couple moments at the Grammys below…

    Heidi Klum and Tom Kaulitz

    Heidi Klum and her husband Tom Kaulitz couldn't take their eyes off each other and looked so in love as they posed for photos while lost in each other's gaze.

    Chrissy Teigen and John Legend

    Chrissy Teigen couldn't keep her hands off her husband, John Legend. The couple were caught giggling and fawning over each other on several occasions.

    Beyonce and Jay Z

    Beyonce and Jay Z were the perfect power couple at the Grammys. The long-term lovebirds, who tied the knot in April 2008, struck a pose as they sat down for dinner.

    Jay was later supported by their 12-year-old daughter Blue Ivy Carter when he took to the stage to receive the Dr. Dre Global Impact Award — an honor recognizing his profound influence on the global music landscape.    

    Miley Cyrus and Maxx Morando

    Miley Cyrus put on a public display of affection with her boyfriend Maxx Morando, locking lips as she celebrated her victory.

    The 'Flowers' singer, who bagged Best Pop Solo Performance and Record of the Year for her pop anthem, wasn't shy about showing her affection for her partner. She didn't hold back in the wardrobe department either, undergoing an impressive five outfit changes throughout the evening.


    Victoria Monet and John Gaines

    Victoria Monet and her partner John Gaines brought their two-year-old daughter Hazel along to the ceremony. The loved-up couple were the picture of family bliss as they posed on the red carpet.

    Little Hazel made history as the youngest person to be nominated for a Grammy Award after receiving a nod alongside Victoria for best traditional R&B performance for the track 'Hollywood'.

    Paris Hilton and Carter Reum

    New parents Paris Hilton and Carter Reum enjoyed a child-free night, posing up a storm as they graced the red carpet arm in arm.

    Hilton Hotel heiress Paris, who shocked fans last autumn when she announced she had welcomed her second child via surrogacy, gazed lovingly into Carter's eyes as he laced a protective hand around his wife's waist.

    Sofia Richie and Elliot Grainge

    Sofia Richie and her husband Elliot Grainge shared a giggle while she cradled her blossoming baby bump.

    Jon Batiste and Suleika Jaouad

    Singer Jon Batiste couldn't stop showing his wife, Suleika Jaouad affection and was captured several times planting a sweet kiss on her cheek.

    Calvin Harris and Vick Hope

    Calvin Harris and his new wife Vick Hope put on a loved-up display by wrapping their arms around each other's waists and beaming for the camera – and how incredible did she look in her gold and tan beaded gown by Australian bridal & evening wear label, KYHA Studios.

    Mark Ronson and Grace Gummer

    There's no denying the look of love Grace Gummer gave her husband, Mark Ronson. Holding hands appeared to be the theme of the night as they too didn't let go of each other while having their pictures taken.

    Billy Joel and Alexis Roderick

    Billy Joel and his wife Alexis Roderick looked as in sync as ever as they adorably held hands while posing for photos.

    Kelly Osbourne and Sid Wilson

    Kelly Osbourne and Slipknot's Sid Wilson began dating in January 2022 after years of friendship – but it took them until the 2024 Grammys to make their red carpet debut.

    The couple looked so sweet as they posed for pictures with their arms around each other, and Kelly's huge smile showed just how happy she was to be by her man's side. 

    David Guetta and Jessica Ledon

    French DJ and producer David Guetta looked so loved-up with his pregnant girlfriend, Jessica Ledon, with the couple – who announced they were expecting in November 2023 – packing on the PDA on the red carpet.

    The hitmaker was pictured sweetly planting a kiss on Jessica's cheek and keeping a protective hand on her growing baby bump.

    Brandi Carlile and Catherine Shepherd

    Grammy-winning artist Brandi Carlile has been with her wife, Catherine Shepherd for over a decade, and their love is still as strong with neither of them letting go of each other's hand as they posed for photos on the carpet.

    Ted Danson and Mary Steenburgen

    Ted Danson and Mary Steenburgen are as adorable now as they were when they first started dating back in 1993!

    Not only did they mirror each other in chic dark outfits, but with their body language too, holding each other close as they posed for photos with huge smiles on their faces.

    Andrew Watt and Charlotte Lawrence

    Grammy-winning music producer Andrew Watt and his girlfriend, singer-songwriter Charlotte Lawrence couldn't keep their hands off each other.

    In-between loving glances and giggles, the couple – who went public with their relationship back in June 2021 – happily kissed in front of the cameras to demonstrate their insatiable love for each other.

    Tanya Blount and Michael Trotter Jr.

    Tanya Blount and Michael Trotter Jr., husband and wife duo of The War and Treaty, looked incredible in their brightly-colored, co-ordinated outfits – and so adorable holding hands on the red carpet.
