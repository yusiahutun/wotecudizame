Early life of Shawn Mendes

Shawn was born on August 8,1998 and was brought up in Pickering ,Ontario . His father Manuel Mendes is a businessman who sells bar and restaurant supplies in Toronto , he is from Algarve , Portugal , while his mother Karen Mendes is a real estate agent and she is from England . Shawn has one younger sister named Aaliyah who was raised in religious family background .

Education of Shawn Mendes

Mendes completed his graduation from Pine Ridge Secondary School in June 2016 . He joined a glee club in his high school where he practised his stage presence in acting lessons . Once he was auditioned for Disney Channel in Toronto.

Career

Add my biography

Mendes at the age of 14 in 2012 learnt to play guitar from youtube tutorial videos , then he started posting song cover on youtube which started gaining followers day by day and it’s pace increased after he posted a cover from Justin Bieber’s ” As Long as You Love Me” on social video app Vine in the year 2013 , on that video he got more than 10,000 likes and gained many followers too. By August 2014 he became the most -followed musician and influencer on Vine app . In following year he grabbed the attention of artist manager  Andrew Gertler and Island Records A&R Ziggy Chareton, who brought him to Island Records in January and was officially signed in May , 2014 .

On June 26 ,2014 , Mendes released his first single ” Life of the Party ” and became the the youngest to debut in the top 25 on the US Billboard Hot 100 by grabbing up 24th position in July 12 ,2014 . Further his second single ” Something Big ” was released on November 6, 2014. On April 14, 2015 he released hi full -lenght album ” Handwritten” which gave a boost to his career .
